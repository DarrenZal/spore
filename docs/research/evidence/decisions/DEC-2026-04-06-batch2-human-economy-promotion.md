## Structural Note: The CLC Exclusion

rather than independent corroboration is itself a consequence-bearing
governance choice. It constrains how CLC's operational evidence can be
used in future Spore work. CLC representatives were not part of this
decision — it was a methodological judgment about evidence independence,
not a stakeholder negotiation.

consequence bearer: CLC's framing in Spore was decided without CLC
participation. Whether this matters depends on whether CLC has a stake
in how Spore characterizes their work — a question this trace surfaces
but does not answer.
