# ADR-0051 — Relational-Identity as Named Property of Holon (Option A)

## Status

active (authored + activated 2026-04-22 under `canon-rebuild-phase-3b-5` decision-gated plan; Option A — `relational-identity` as named property of Holon; strict relational-only scope per capstone §7; slug `relational-identity` primary + `identity-as-relational` alias — per operator selection post decision-brief)

## Context

Phase 3b.1 (Care, ADR-0045), Phase 3b.2 (Norms, ADR-0046), Phase 3b.3 (Power allocational, ADR-0047), Phase 3b.3b (Johar three-form / modes-across-primitives, ADR-0048), Phase 3b.4 (Reproduction-continuity, ADR-0049), and Phase 3b.5a (Sociality-reducibility, ADR-0050) closed same-day 2026-04-22. Phase 3b.5 closes the last Tier-2 item from capstone §3: the `identity` row.

### Capstone verdict (single-limb, explicit)

**§3 Identity row** (lines 70–72, verbatim HEAD `43f1d2e`):

> *"Tradition-count: load-bearing in Viable System Model (S5 is identity), Care Ethics (relational self), Pluriversal (kinship), Distributed Systems (cryptographic for BFT), Trust Networks (identity-persistence — Friedman-Resnick), Governance-Process (Apache 'individuals, not organizations'), Autopoiesis / 4E (Barandiaran-Di Paolo-Rohde), Agent-Based Modeling, commoning-Commons; thin in Philosophy of Collective Agency, MARL, REA/VF (AgentRelationship deferred), Category Theory, Canonical Layering (auth/auth), Repo-Topology (ownership); absent in Audience-Scoping, Structured Disagreement.*
> *Structural role: substrate in VSM; agent-property in Distributed/Trust; relational in Care/Pluriversal.*
> *Paraphrase test: partial. Persistent holon property + signed signals covers VSM and Distributed cases. Pluriversal kinship-identity irreducible.*
> *Verdict: admit as a named property of holon, not a separate primitive."*

**§1 convergence 4** (line 20, verbatim):

> *"Identity is relational / constituted through participation, not pre-given. Supporting traditions: Care Ethics ('ties that partly constitute our identity'); Pluriversal / Indigenous Governance (personhood via kinship with more-than-human); Commons Governance (Bollier & Helfrich's 'Nested-I'); Autopoiesis / 4E Cognition; Viable System Model (identity as S5's defining function); Philosophy of Collective Agency (List/Pettit functional group agents; Tuomela ethos). Dissenters: MARL; Distributed Systems (identity as cryptographic tag); Category Theory / Sheaves (identity morphism only). Why this matters for Spore: Holon presupposes a relational identity story; permission to treat identity as constituted-by-commitment-and-care rather than given-and-tagged."*

**§7 frozen-concepts Appendix** (line 259, verbatim):

> *"identity-as-relational (new) — 5 load-bearing in Care Ethics, Pluriversal, commoning-Commons, Autopoiesis / 4E, and Viable System Model. Not a separate primitive; a named property rather than a standalone primitive. This count excludes cryptographic-tag, persistent-handle, and authorship/sponsorship identity readings, which belong to a broader `identity` bucket rather than this relational subset. Candidate slug: `relational-identity` (distinct from cryptographic-identity tag reading)."*

### Structural scaffolding from Phase 3b.5a (precursor-to-3b.5)

ADR-0050 extended Holon primitive with an explicit irreducibility clause (live canon at `docs/project-vision.md` L28):

> *"The whole is not reducible to an aggregation of parts (ADR-0050): a holon exhibits organizational invariants and emergent properties that cannot be analytically recovered from constituent-analysis alone (List/Pettit supervenience-without-reducibility, Group Agency 2011; Thompson dynamic co-emergence, Mind in Life 2007). ADR-0016 established the holon/field distinction; ADR-0050 makes explicit the irreducibility clause ADR-0016 was agnostic on."*

This is the structural home for the capstone verdict's "named property of holon." The five load-bearing traditions each describe the-whole-emergent-property from different angles:

- **VSM (Beer 1984):** identity as what S5 closes the recursion on — *"only this determines an identity"*; not a property of any individual part.
- **Care Ethics (Held, Tronto, Baier):** identity as constituted-by-relations — *"personal identity and moral agency are constituted by ties and responsibilities to others."* The relations, not the relata, carry identity.
- **Pluriversal / Indigenous Governance (de la Cadena, Plumwood, Escobar):** kinship-identity — a person is constituted-by-kin-tie (with other humans, ancestors, land, more-than-human beings), not logically prior to it.
- **Autopoiesis / 4E Cognition (Maturana-Varela, Thompson, Barandiaran-Di Paolo-Rohde):** identity as structural-coupling between organism and environment; neither pole carries identity alone.
- **Commons Governance (Bollier-Helfrich):** *Nested-I* — self-as-nested-in-commons-practice, constituted through commoning.

All five are descriptions-of-the-whole-emergent-layer — the property that ADR-0050 made canon and ADR-0051 now names.

Separately, project-vision.md L62 (authored by ADR-0050 close-out) already signposts this disposition:

> *"Phase 3b.5a has shaped the decision-space for Phase 3b.5 Identity: relational-identity now has a canon-structural home at Holon's whole-emergent-layer (Side-B commitment + Holon irreducibility extension per ADR-0050)."*

ADR-0051 executes the disposition the signpost anticipates.

### Identity-bucket scope (capstone §7 discipline)

Per capstone §7 line 259, the broader `identity` bucket contains four readings; only one is in ADR-0051 scope:

| Identity reading | Canonical home | ADR-0051 scope |
|---|---|---|
| **Relational-identity** (Care, Pluriversal, Commons, Autopoiesis/4E, VSM) | Holon whole-emergent-property (admitted here) | ✅ IN SCOPE |
| **Cryptographic-identity** (Distributed BFT, Trust Networks) | Membrane signing-function; federation-protocol signing discipline | ❌ out of scope; coexisting |
| **Persistent-handle identity** (Trust Networks pseudonym-stability) | Federation-protocol persistence; KOI entity-resolution (tier-2 fuzzy + tier-3 semantic) | ❌ out of scope; coexisting |
| **Authorship/sponsorship identity** (Repo-Topology CODEOWNERS; Apache "individuals not organizations") | Implicit in Commitment party-composition + Evidence attestor-field | ❌ out of scope; coexisting |

ADR-0051 does NOT author an integrated four-bucket `§Identity-taxonomy`. The three non-relational buckets retain their existing implicit handling and are acknowledged as distinct-but-coexisting without re-disposition.

## Decision

**Admit `relational-identity` as a named property of the Holon primitive.**

Canon-body edits:
1. **`docs/project-vision.md`** — Holon primitive bullet (§Structural primitives) is extended with a relational-identity property paragraph, immediately following the existing irreducibility clause (ADR-0050) since the two statements structurally compose (ADR-0050 establishes the whole-emergent-layer; ADR-0051 names the property at that layer). The extension articulates the 5-tradition load-bearing basis (VSM S5-closure / Care constitutive-relations / Pluriversal kinship-constitution / Autopoiesis structural-coupling / Commons Nested-I) and maps paraphrase-test residue (Pluriversal kinship-identity) to ADR-0001 held-tension cluster.
2. **`docs/foundations/governance-artifacts-and-graph-projections.md`** — The parallel "Structural primitives host the verbs" paragraph is updated to match project-vision.md: relational-identity added as Holon property alongside the irreducibility clause.
3. **`docs/project-vision.md` L62** (deferred-candidates line): Identity closure is moved from "decision-space shaped" (3b.5a signposting) to "closed by ADR-0051."
4. **`docs/research/concepts-p2p-wiki.yaml`** — Version bump v8→v9 admitting `relational-identity` as primary slug with alias `identity-as-relational`. Both slugs are admitted to frozen-vocab; bridge-note authors may reference either.

Slug form:
- **Primary: `relational-identity`** — matches Spore frozen-vocab convention of modifier-first compound slugs (cf. `asymmetric-commitment`, `joint-commitment`, `asymmetric-membrane`, `reproductive-commoning`, `care-commoning`).
- **Alias: `identity-as-relational`** — preserves capstone §7 frontmatter form for citability and link-compatibility with capstone cross-references.

Canonical-label: **Relational Identity** (capstone-lineage; not "Identity-as-Relational" which is the predicative-qualifier form).

One-line definition: The whole-emergent-property of a Holon that is constituted through relations — ties, kinship, structural coupling, commons-nesting, recursion-closure — rather than carried by any individual part; capstone §3 admitted as named property of Holon, not as separate primitive; 5 load-bearing traditions (Care Ethics, Pluriversal/Indigenous Governance, Commons Governance, Autopoiesis/4E, Viable System Model).

Scope discipline: strict relational-only. Cryptographic / persistent-handle / authorship identity-readings coexist as distinct buckets in their existing canonical homes without re-disposition.

### Paraphrase-test residue (verdict: partial, per capstone)

Per capstone §3 line 71: *"Paraphrase test: partial. Persistent holon property + signed signals covers VSM and Distributed cases. Pluriversal kinship-identity irreducible."*

- **VSM case captured.** S5-as-identity maps to Holon's relational-identity property — the recursion-closure is named canon-legibly as a property of the whole-emergent-layer.
- **Distributed Systems case captured elsewhere.** "Signed signals" operates at Membrane + federation-protocol signing discipline, not at relational-identity. Cryptographic-identity remains in its existing canonical home; capstone's paraphrase-test frames relational-identity + signed-signals as jointly covering VSM + Distributed.
- **Care Ethics case captured.** Held/Tronto's constitutive-relations identity is relational-identity directly.
- **Commons (Nested-I) case captured.** Bollier-Helfrich self-as-nested-in-commons maps to Holon-participating-in-containing-fields composition.
- **Autopoiesis/4E case captured.** Structural-coupling-as-identity maps to Holon's whole-emergent-property (coupling is between Holon and its environment across Membrane; the identity lives at the Holon-as-whole layer).
- **Pluriversal kinship-identity — partial / residue.** Capstone flags as "irreducible." ADR-0051 captures the property-frame (personhood-as-kinship-constitution IS a whole-emergent-property) while preserving ADR-0001 held-tension for the ontological commitments (one-world-vs-many-worlds) that a single canonical property-definition cannot force. Pluriversal kinship-identity operates compatibly under BOTH frames: (i) canon-posture — relational-identity as Holon whole-emergent-property; (ii) held-tension — ADR-0001 pluriversal-incommensurability cluster preserving ontological alternatives. This dual-operability pattern is direct pattern-reuse from ADR-0050's handling of pluriversal-joint-commitment (*"operate compatibly under both canon-posture (ADR-0050 primitive) and held-tension frame (ADR-0001)"* — project-vision.md L40).

### Identity-bucket coexistence (scope discipline)

Strict relational-only per capstone §7. The three non-relational identity-readings remain in their existing canonical homes:

1. **Cryptographic-identity:** Membrane signing-function + federation-protocol signing discipline. Not re-disposed.
2. **Persistent-handle identity:** Federation-protocol persistence + KOI entity-resolution (tier-2 fuzzy + tier-3 semantic). Not re-disposed.
3. **Authorship/sponsorship identity:** Implicit in Commitment party-composition + Evidence attestor-field. Not re-disposed.

No `§Identity-taxonomy` integrated framework authored in project-vision.md body. (Option G rejected — operator directive; Option D rejected on scope-bleed.) Future integrated treatment remains parked in decision-brief §Parking for separate plan if collaborative need emerges.

### Novel move-pattern: "property-of-primitive" as sub-specification

ADR-0051 instantiates a **property-of-primitive** move-pattern: naming a canon-legible property of an existing primitive that is NOT itself a primitive / doctrine / mode / pattern. This parallels Field's rule-stratification (ADR-0046) as sub-stratification-within-a-primitive — *internal-structure-of-primitive* articulated via ADR prose referencing the primitive's parent ADR without file-editing. The canon-review-protocol v2 documents four move-patterns (new-primitive admission; doctrine-layer admission; extend-existing-primitive; mode-across-primitives); "property-of-primitive as sub-specification" is a fifth. Naming this pattern explicitly in protocol v3 or separate harvest is parked as a method-protocol artifact (out of 3b.5 scope).
