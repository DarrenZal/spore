# ADR-0077 — Actor-Governance Foundation Doc Promotion

## Status

active (authored + activated 2026-04-25 under canon-rebuild Phase 4 Tier B FINAL admission; Tier B closes with this ADR after F6 ADR-0075 and F5 ADR-0076 landed same session)

## Context

Phase 4 of the canon-rebuild arc was defined in the 2026-04-22 canon-first-principles-audit-v2 (`tmp/canon-first-principles-audit-v2-2026-04-22.md` §6.4, lines 352–362) as "7–9 new foundation docs, priority-ordered." The 2026-04-25 Phase 4 scoping session ratified three-tier admission sequencing: Tier A (F1 + F4, landed 2026-04-25 as ADR-0073 and ADR-0074), Tier B (F6 + F5 + F3 per operator-inverted ordering — invention-heavy before synthesis-heavy), Tier C (F7 + F8 + F2 + F9).

F3 actor-governance is the priority-3 missing foundation per audit-v2 §6.4 item 3 (Codex + Opus-4-7 convergence; revised to priority-2 in Phase 4 scoping per substrate maturity). Three already-landed canon artifacts carry explicit forward-references to F3 as Phase 4 canon-authoring target:

1. **ADR-0042 §Consequences L82** (`docs/research/canon-decisions/0042-dag-delete-structural-legitimacy-promote.md`): *"Phase 4's Actor Governance foundation doc (per v2 §6.4) builds on this coupling claim."* — direct foundation-doc handoff anticipated since 2026-04-22.
2. **F6 failure-modes §4.7** (`docs/foundations/failure-modes.md:161`): *"Actor-capture-specific governance doctrine — who has standing to contest a captured actor, who adjudicates, what the recall / replacement / intervention protocols are — is the scope of F3 actor-governance (forthcoming)."* — F6.7 commits to category-recognition; F3 commits to governance-response across all four sub-shapes.
3. **F5 actuator-logic §4.2 R2-contest** (`docs/foundations/actuator-logic.md`): *"F5 §4.2 (contest) forward-references F3 for actor-capture-specific response per F6.7 pairing. F3 will carry governance-doctrine (standing, adjudication, recall, replacement)."*

Plus audit-v2 §3.3 item 4 (Codex + Opus-4-7 primary formulation): *"Actor governance vs artifact governance — canon governs artifacts; real multi-agent decision governance is unvalidated. Who decides under contest? How is standing granted? What authority cycles are valid? How do human and AI agents differ in rights/responsibilities?"* — four explicit framing questions.

### Substrate density unique to F3

F3 sits atop the densest substrate of any Phase-4 admission. Eight prior ADRs and three foundation docs supply substrate at varying maturity:

- **ADR-0042 structural-legitimacy** — coupling-to-consequence as legitimacy ground; ADR-0042 explicitly anticipates F3 builds atop this coupling claim.
- **ADR-0046 Ostrom 3-level rule-stack** — operational / collective-choice / constitutional rule-levels; permissive offer to actor-governance work at line 225.
- **ADR-0047 Power-decomposition Layers 1/2/3** — authority-over-rule-levels (Field) / asymmetric-commitment (Commitment) / asymmetric-membrane (Membrane); F3 operationalizes all three at actor layer.
- **ADR-0050 joint-commitment primitive** — Gilbertian joint-commitment; F3 governs joint-actors at federation/organization scale.
- **ADR-0061 asymmetric-joint-commitment slug-decline** — composition-pattern (ADR-0050 + ADR-0047 Layer-2) carries the load; F3 §4.3 implements.
- **ADR-0068 federation-encounter composition-pattern** — encounter as venue for actor-coordination; F3 §4.5 governs actors-within-encounter.
- **F6.7 actor-capture sub-taxonomy** — 4 sub-shapes ready for F3-side governance-response discharge.
- **F5 §4.2 R2-contest forward-ref** — actor-disputes route to F3.
- **F4 §5.3 Appeal-Protocol** — inter-layer adjudication routing F3 cites wholesale.

This substrate density is what makes F3 **synthesis-heavy** in operator-inverted Tier B ordering rationale — rich substrate makes integration the central authoring concern, distinct from F6 (invention-heavy, no prior taxonomy) and F5 (mid-weight, inheriting F1+F4+F6 + re-converging on B1).

### B5 novel B-axis (selective per-category synthesis-depth)

Per Step 0.5 audit (`tmp/adr-0077-f3-audit-manifest-2026-04-25.md` §F): F3's substrate is **structurally heterogeneous across categories**. Three categories (admission, actor-capture-remediation, governance-body-composition) have NO prior canon substrate at the doctrinal layer — net-new substrate to author HEAVY. Five categories (authority-delegation, power-asymmetry, joint-actor, federation-encounter-actor, authorization-boundaries) inherit rich substrate from ADRs 0042/0046/0047/0050/0068 + F4 §5.3 — appropriate-depth is SELECTIVE cite-and-operationalize.

Forcing B1 unified principled-rule across 8 categories would invent fake symmetry across heterogeneous substrate. Forcing B2 per-category would re-author canon-prior substrate, violating F5's cite-don't-redefine discipline. **B5 SELECTIVE per-category** is the honest-rigor shape: each category-row picks synthesis-depth based on substrate maturity at that category's target-layer.

This is the FIFTH B-axis disposition observed in Phase 4 (B1 F1 unified / B1 F4 unified-with-extension / B2 F6 per-category / B1 F5 re-converged-unified / B5 F3 selective-per-category). The B-axis sequence validates template-adaptability as substrate-driven discipline at full extension; B5 is novel in this admission and reusable for any future foundation doc whose substrate is heterogeneous across its categories.

### Operator-inverted Tier B ordering — F3 as connective tissue

Per F6 ADR-0075 §Method-precedent 1 + F5 ADR-0076 inheritance: Tier B admissions can be ordered by cognitive-load (invention-heavy before synthesis-heavy) when forward-ref discipline is load-bearing. F6 (invention-heavy) and F5 (mid-weight) landed before F3 (synthesis-heavy). F3 closes Tier B by discharging the forward-refs F6 and F5 surfaced — multi-forward-ref cluster-discharge in single admission (method-precedent 3 below).

ADR-0042 (dag-delete + structural-legitimacy-promote, 2026-04-22) is the authoritative template for foundation-doc promotion via ADR, inherited by ADR-0073 (F1), ADR-0074 (F4), ADR-0075 (F6), ADR-0076 (F5). ADR-0077 inherits the five-part coordinated-admission pattern (ADR + new foundation doc + canon-review-protocol §1 registration + docs/README.md registration + concepts yaml slug admission) directly.
