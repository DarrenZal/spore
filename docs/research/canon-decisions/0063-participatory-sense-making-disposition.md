### Step 0.5 audit: nine operational cases

The earning-test evaluation enumerated nine per-instance-family Signal operational cases and classified each as participatory-sense-making-supporting, sender-receiver-supporting, or ambiguous dual-phase:

1. **BKC commons pool-formation deliberation** (PSM-supporting MODERATE). When BKC members collectively deliberate the scope of a new commitment pool, meaning about what the pool IS and what it covers emerges progressively in deliberative exchange — no single party transmits a pre-formed pool-definition. PSM framing fits deliberation-process; once the pool is formed, signal/evidence mechanics resume in sender-receiver mode. BKC evidence is hackathon/demo-level per project working-rules, not local-production evidence.

2. **Federation protocol-version negotiation** (ambiguous, dual-phase). Pre-adoption negotiation involves joint meaning-making about what a new spec means for each member's commitments (PSM). Post-adoption dissemination is sender-receiver transmission of the ratified protocol. The primitive-level choice between PSM and SR depends on which phase of the lifecycle you privilege. Structural parallel to ADR-0062 Membrane dual-mode.

3. **IC memory-governance curation conversations** (PSM-supporting STRONG-conceptually). The bridge note `4e-cognition-and-participatory-sense-making.md` §4 explicitly frames IC governance as *"participatory sense-making across memory-governance boundaries — the dyadic (or n-adic) interaction process by which two or more memory-layers, agents, or knowledge commons co-enact shared understanding."* The PSM framing is operationally load-bearing for how collective decisions about what gets preserved, attributed, and linked proceed — the interaction dynamic itself is the governance surface.

4. **PM matchmaking dialogue** (ambiguous, weak PSM). `pm:Intent` is pre-formed and transmitted for matching (SR primary at the protocol layer); dialogue can refine and co-specify `pm:CommitmentBundle` scope before finalization (PSM at the relational layer). PM grammar.md has no explicit enactivist language; the protocol as-designed is SR-primary.

5. **Octo agent autonomous research** (SR-supporting MODERATE). Octo's agent-architecture is agent-to-environment in solo mode (no interaction) and agent-to-collaborators via signals (broadcast/query patterns) in federation mode. Neither mode satisfies PSM's dyadic mutual constitution of meaning; autonomous-research coordination fits SR framing better.

6. **Joint-commitment formation processes** (PSM-supporting STRONG-conceptually, per ADR-0050 paradigm). Gilbert's *form-joint-commitment* operation (open expression of readiness under common knowledge per ADR-0050) is paradigmatically PSM: each party's expression of readiness is perturbed by and perturbs the other's; the joint-commitment form exists only in the interaction, not in either party alone. PSM and joint-commitment share the "not reducible to sum of individuals" structure — but ADR-0050 (line 141) explicitly treats them as different Side-B positions (Gilbertian obligation-generating vs. enactive meaning-emerging). Operational convergence is real; canonical status is distinct.

7. **Spec-DAG amendment discussions** (ambiguous, dual-phase). Pre-text deliberation exhibits PSM framing; the produced governance artifact is text-authoritative (ADR-0041) and circulates in SR mode post-authoring. Same dual-phase shape as Case 2.

8. **Signal-as-algedonic-bypass** (SR-supporting STRONG). Algedonic signals (VSM — bypass hierarchy to surface urgency) are sender-receiver in the most canonical sense: a unit signals variance to the regulator, bypassing the normal reporting chain. Unilateral transmission by design; PSM framing is structurally inapplicable.

9. **Stigmergic trace coordination** (SR-supporting MODERATE). Stigmergy (indirect coordination through environmental modification — pheromones, shared data-structure traces) is not dyadic constitutive meaning-making. Agents respond to traces left by others without direct dyadic interaction; SR fits better than PSM, though neither is a perfect fit.

Aggregate classification: **3 PSM-supporting / 3 SR-supporting / 3 ambiguous dual-phase.**

### Tradition-citation evaluation: the single-tradition-support concern

The plan's authoritative rule on tradition-breadth (for overcoming the ADR-0053 R-Sig-1 single-tradition-support block) requires ≥2 distinct outside-enactivist cluster-counts for primitive admission (Option a) and ≥2 cluster-equivalents total for lighter admission categories (derived slug / framing-note / scope-condition). The 5-cluster taxonomy:

**Cluster A — Enactivist** (counts as 1 full cluster):
- Primary: De Jaegher & Di Paolo (2007) "Participatory Sense-Making" *Phenomenology and the Cognitive Sciences* 6(4): 485–507; De Jaegher, Di Paolo & Gallagher (2010) "Can social interaction constitute social cognition?" *TiCS* 14(10): 441–447.
- Synthesis: Thompson (2007) *Mind in Life: Biology, Phenomenology, and the Sciences of Mind* (Harvard).
- Lineage: Maturana & Varela (1980) *Autopoiesis and Cognition*; Varela, Thompson & Rosch (1991) *The Embodied Mind* (MIT).
- Extension: Di Paolo, Cuffari & De Jaegher (2018) *Linguistic Bodies* (MIT); Gallagher (2017) *Enactivist Interventions* (Oxford), Chs. 7–8.
- Social extension: Fuchs & De Jaegher (2009) intersubjectivity paper.
- In-repo: `4e-cognition-and-participatory-sense-making.md` bridge note (entire); `research-autopoiesis-4e.md` corpus review §§42–46; `autopoiesis-and-structural-coupling.md` bridge note; concepts yaml slug `enactive-cognition` (v2+); ADR-0053 R-Sig-1 citation.

**Cluster B — Dialogical / Social-constructionist** (partial convergence on family-claim; does NOT count as full independent cluster):
- Bakhtin (1981) *The Dialogic Imagination* — dialogism: meaning as addressee-shaped utterance-constituted; every utterance anticipates response. Family-claim support for "meaning is social/intersubjective." Does NOT arrive at interaction-process operational autonomy.
- Vygotsky (1978) *Mind in Society* — intermental → intramental sequence: higher cognitive functions arise in social interaction before internalization. Family-claim support for "cognition is socially constituted." Different mechanism (developmental priority, not interaction-process autonomy).
- Harré & Gillett (1994) *The Discursive Mind* — discursive psychology: personhood as enacted-in-discourse. Closest to Cluster A in anti-representationalism.
- Mead (1934) *Mind, Self, and Society* — symbolic interactionism: self arises via taking-perspective-of-the-other. Shares anti-individualism; different mechanism (role-taking, not structural coupling).

**Cluster C — Actor-Network / Distributed Cognition** (partial convergence; does NOT count as full independent cluster):
- Hutchins (1995) *Cognition in the Wild* — distributed cognition: cognition distributed across people, tools, and artifacts; unit of analysis is the sociotechnical system. Anti-individualist unit-of-analysis support; does not arrive at dyadic interaction-process operational autonomy.
- Latour & Woolgar (1979) *Laboratory Life*; Callon (1986) "Some Elements of a Sociology of Translation" — actor-network theory: translations reshape actors in the process of association. Distant from PSM's dyadic focus.
- Clark & Chalmers (1998) "The Extended Mind" *Analysis* 58(1): 7–19 — extended cognition; cognitive processes extend beyond the skull. External-scaffolding focus, not interaction-emergence.

**Cluster D — Phenomenological Intersubjectivity** (absorbed into Cluster A via Thompson; partial independent support only):
- Husserl (1931) *Cartesian Meditations* §§42–54 — intersubjectivity constituted through analogical apperception; transcendental intersubjectivity grounds shared world. Largely absorbed into Cluster A through Thompson's *Mind in Life* synthesis.
- Merleau-Ponty (1962) *Phenomenology of Perception* Part III — intercorporeality; pre-reflective bodily intersubjectivity. Also absorbed into Cluster A.
- Schutz (1932) *The Phenomenology of the Social World* — we-relations as directly experienced face-to-face situating as primordial social encounter. Independent sociological-phenomenological tradition not absorbed into enactivism.

**Cluster E — Language-Games / Practice-Theory** (partial convergence on family-claim; does NOT count as full independent cluster):
- Wittgenstein (1953) *Philosophical Investigations* — language-games as forms of life; meaning-as-use in practice; private-language argument forecloses individual-alone meaning. Supports anti-private-meaning family claim; focus on rule-following and practice, not dyadic interaction autonomy. Absorbed partially into Di Paolo et al. (2018) *Linguistic Bodies*.
- Bourdieu (1980) *The Logic of Practice* — habitus, practice theory: embodied pre-reflective enactments reproduce social life. Anti-representationalism support; focus on reproduction-through-practice, not dyadic meaning-emergence.

**Honest count**: 1.0 full cluster (Enactivist) + ~2.0 partial cluster-equivalents (B/C/D/E contributing ~0.5 each to family-claim support without independently arriving at PSM's specific mechanism). Total breadth score ~3.0 with generous counting; strict counting: 1 full cluster + 4 partial convergences.

**Threshold assessment per plan rule**:
- Primitive admission (Option a): ≥2 distinct outside-enactivist full clusters REQUIRED. **NOT MET** — adjacent traditions converge on family-claim ("meaning is intersubjective") but not on PSM-specific mechanism ("interaction process acquires operational closure and autonomy per De Jaegher & Di Paolo"). They cluster as supportive background, not as independent convergent arrivals at PSM.
- Derived slug / property / scope-condition (lighter categories): ≥2 cluster-equivalents total REQUIRED. **MARGINALLY MET** — Cluster A (1.0) + partial support across B/C/D/E (~2.0 total partials) clears the threshold for lighter admission categories.

Single-tradition-support concern from ADR-0053 R-Sig-1 is **maintained for primitive admission** and **marginally overcome for lighter dispositions**.

### ADR-0050 Side-B canonical commitment relationship

ADR-0050 committed Spore to Side-B in the sociality-reducibility question via three coordinated moves: (1) Holon irreducibility (whole not reducible to aggregation of parts), (2) canonicalization of the Phase 3b.1–4 Side-B structural tilt, (3) `joint-commitment` admitted as Spore's 9th primitive. ADR-0050 explicitly noted (line 141): *"the enactive form remains accessible as mode-of-joint-commitment-operation (future canon could clarify if operational pressure warrants)"* and (line 60 in project-vision.md): *"Other Side-B primitive candidates (participatory-sense-making per De Jaegher/Di Paolo 2007; we-mode per Tuomela PoS 2007; Searle we-intention 1990) remain accessible as cited-lineage or alternative-positions."*

PSM is the *enactive* form of Side-B (sense-making constitutively interactive); `joint-commitment` is the *Gilbertian* form (commitment constitutively joint). The two forms are parallel articulations of the same sociality-is-irreducibly-collective commitment, operating at different layers: joint-commitment at the obligation-layer (binding; demand-rights; rescindable-only-by-concurrence); PSM at the meaning-layer (sense-making; operational closure of the interaction process; mutual incorporation). ADR-0063 articulates this parallel-articulation explicitly in canon-body prose at Signal-bullet without admitting PSM as an additional Side-B primitive.

The relationship to joint-commitment is load-bearing: in Case 6 (joint-commitment formation), PSM is operationally relevant as the enactive characterization of *how* form-joint-commitment proceeds. This is precisely ADR-0050 line 141's "mode-of-joint-commitment-operation" framing. Canon now names the parallelism explicitly; no new canon-object-class is introduced.

### ADR-0053 §3 Signal precedent relationship

ADR-0053 (Phase 3b.7) established the canonical home of the enactive-objection acknowledgment at project-vision.md:65 and governance-artifacts.md:50 (stance-template prose: *"signal names a coordination-grammar primitive at the operational sender-receiver layer... the autopoietic structural-coupling reduction operates at the cognitive-science / enactive-grounding layer and stands as a principled standing objection that does not defeat operational signal-as-primitive"*). ADR-0063 **DEEPENS** this precedent by performing the dedicated earning-test evaluation that R-Sig-1 deferred and closing the holding-open with formal disposition. The Signal bullet's autopoiesis-objection acknowledgment is unchanged; ADR-0063 adds a companion sub-bullet on sense-making-mode scope that articulates PSM positively for the cases where it is operationally relevant and names SR as operationally sufficient for the remaining cases.

### Field-Signal internal-consistency assessment

The Field-Membrane internal-consistency tension ADR-0062 addressed (capstone §5 line 185: *"if Spore takes autopoiesis seriously (which its field primitive implies), membrane should become produced-by-the-holon"*) has a parallel at Signal: if Field is autopoiesis-resonant in some readings, should Signal be PSM-exclusively rather than dual-mode? ADR-0062's verdict applies: Field is Ostromian-constitutive per ADR-0046, not strictly Maturana-Varela autopoietic. Signal's dual-mode scope is internally consistent with Field's Ostromian-constitutive scope and Membrane's dual-production-mode scope. The scoped-grammar commitment (project-vision.md §Scope and frame acknowledgment: *"This is a scoped grammar, not a universal account of coordination"*) supports cross-primitive asymmetry on the autopoiesis-active / enactive reading.

### Earning-test verdict

**(Q-a) Protocol-surface specifiability**: MARGINAL. De Jaegher & Di Paolo (2007) and Fuchs & De Jaegher (2009) specify interaction-process operations (entry/exit conditions, cadence, mutual incorporation, breakdown-and-repair) at the cognitive-science layer. The bridge note `4e-cognition-and-participatory-sense-making.md` §4 proposes these as IC-governance surface operations but does not yet cross into protocol specification. The corpus review `research-autopoiesis-4e.md` §"Where the tradition is thin" names the characteristic weakness: *"Trust, commitment, and explicit authority/power have no systematic enactive treatment. De Jaegher's autonomy-in-interaction condition gestures at power but is not a theory of institutional authority. The closest sustained treatment is Gallagher's work on social institutions in Enactivist Interventions Chapters 9–10, but it remains programmatic."* PSM does not carry the same claim as ADR-0049's Reproduction-continuity Q-a marginality (where "the thinness IS the invisibilization phenomenon" was a load-bearing Federicist argument because reproductive labor IS structurally invisible and naming-it creates protocol-surface) — PSM's thinness at Spore's operational layer is a real absence of protocol specification, not an invisible-labor naming.

**(Q-b) Multi-scale operational implementations**: PARTIAL. Three supporting cases (BKC pool-formation deliberation + IC memory-governance curation + joint-commitment formation) + three clear sender-receiver counter-cases (algedonic + stigmergic + Octo) + three ambiguous dual-phase cases. Does not meet ADR-0049/0050 admission standard (four-plus strong cases) but does establish genuine partial operational support — parallel to ADR-0062's 2 + 3 + 3 Membrane-case audit pattern.

Joint-passage test (ADR-0044 lines 98–99: *"A primitive enters the grammar by passing two conditions jointly"*) NOT MET for slug, property, or primitive admission. Per ADR-0048 parsimony-as-earning-test-outcome-not-axiom discipline, canon does not force admission — but also does not force pure decline when the operational reality is genuinely partial and dual-phase.

## Decision

**Option (i) adopt-with-scope-conditioning, with sub-option i.1 (prose-only, no slug, no framing-note).**

Operator selected Option (i) at Step-2 decision-gate. Rationale: the Signal primitive operationally spans both sense-making-modes in current Spore usage; a property or slug admission would over-claim PSM's applicability to all signal-instances (false for algedonic / stigmergic / dissemination cases), and a pure decline would lose the positive articulation of genuine BKC / IC / joint-commitment-formation PSM operational reality. Scope-conditioning prose at the Signal-bullet level is the honest description of a primitive whose operational instances span two distinct sense-making-modes.

The scope-conditioning shape uses **principled-rule** (sender-receiver-transmission vs. constitutively-interactive-emergence) with 2–3 parenthetical example instances per side and dual-phase case acknowledgment. This extends ADR-0062's scope-conditioning pattern to a second primitive. No slug admission, no property admission, no new canon-object-class.

- No slug admission: `participatory-sense-making` is NOT added to concepts-p2p-wiki.yaml. Concepts yaml holds at v12. Tradition-citation breadth is only marginally met for lighter admission categories; over-engineering risk preferred avoidance.
- No property admission: the property-on-primitive count holds at 2 (holon-irreducibility + relational-identity, both on Holon per ADR-0050/0051).
- No framing-note: the dual-phase finding lives at Signal specifically; Signal-bullet scope-conditioning keeps the canonical home with the primitive.
- No new canon-object-class: the four-category inventory (primitives / cross-cutting doctrines / modes-across-primitives / properties-on-primitives) is preserved.

### Method-precedent contribution

ADR-0063 is the **second application of the primitive-bullet scope-conditioning pattern** that ADR-0062 established. The pattern now operates across two structural dimensions of two primitives:
- **Membrane production-mode** (ADR-0062): self-produced (ongoing commoning/stewardship/care labor) vs. text-authoritative (declarative by specification)
- **Signal sense-making-mode** (ADR-0063): sender-receiver-transmission vs. constitutively-interactive-emergence

Both applications resolve ADR-0053 residues — R-Mem-1 (autopoiesis-active reading of Membrane) via ADR-0062; R-Sig-1 (participatory-sense-making as deeper Side-B answer to enactive critique of Signal) via ADR-0063. Both residues flagged canonical-layering-vs-enactive tension-shapes; both are resolved via primitive-bullet scope-conditioning. Method-coherence is explicit: same enactive-vs-canonical-layering tension-shape, same resolution pattern. ADR-0063 confirms primitive-bullet scope-conditioning as a reliable canon-shift shape when a concept operationally spans some-but-not-all primitive instances.

The `decision: scope-condition` frontmatter value (first introduced in ADR-0062) is retained as the canonically-descriptive value. Validator does not enforce specific `decision:` enumerations (confirmed at ADR-0062 Step 5).

### ADR-0050 Side-B PARALLEL articulation

ADR-0063 records a canon-architectural claim that `joint-commitment` (Gilbertian form per ADR-0050) and `participatory-sense-making` (enactive form per De Jaegher & Di Paolo per ADR-0063 cited-lineage) are **parallel articulations** of Spore's Side-B canonical commitment, not competing or sequential primitives. The parallelism operates at different layers:
- `joint-commitment` binds at the obligation-layer (form-joint-commitment / rescind-by-concurrence / hold-accountable-via-demand-right / extend-joint-commitment).
- PSM characterizes meaning-emergence at the sense-making-layer (entry/exit; cadence; mutual incorporation; repair).

In joint-commitment-formation cases (ADR-0050 paradigm), PSM describes HOW form-joint-commitment proceeds in practice (the shared expression of readiness under common knowledge is paradigmatically PSM), while joint-commitment describes WHAT is produced (a distinct coordination binding with demand-rights). The relationship is complementary, not competing. ADR-0050 line 141's "mode-of-joint-commitment-operation" framing is hereby made canon-legible via Signal-bullet scope-conditioning prose.

The capstone §8 Tier-3 item 16 holding-open is closed with formal disposition.

- **Primitive-bullet scope-conditioning pattern confirmed across two primitives.** ADR-0062 established the pattern on Membrane; ADR-0063 confirms it on Signal. Two applications with structurally parallel audit-shapes (2-3 supporting / 3 counter / 3 ambiguous dual-mode or dual-phase) and identical resolution (prose-only scope-conditioning, no slug, no property, no new canon-object-class). The pattern is now a reliable canon-shift shape for partial-evidence + dual-mode cases.

- **R-Sig-1 + R-Mem-1 closed via the same canon-method pattern.** Both ADR-0053 residues that flagged canonical-layering-vs-enactive tensions are now canon-legibly addressed via primitive-bullet scope-conditioning. Method-coherence: same enactive-vs-canonical-layering tension-shape, same scope-conditioning resolution.

- **ADR-0050 Side-B parallel-articulation made canon-legible.** The relationship between `joint-commitment` (Gilbertian) and `participatory-sense-making` (enactive) as parallel Side-B framings of the same canonical commitment is now explicit in canon-body prose. ### Re-visit conditions

ADR-0063 records a scope-conditioning verdict. It does not close the concept permanently. Canon may revisit if:

- (R-0063-1) A non-enactivist tradition develops an operationally-specified definition of interaction-process-as-autonomous-unit with protocol surface (entry/exit governance, cadence governance, mutual-incorporation governance, breakdown-and-repair governance) that Spore's operational layer can absorb. This would satisfy the ≥2-outside-enactivist-clusters tradition-breadth threshold per plan rule.

- (R-0063-2) Three or more new operational cases emerge where PSM is load-bearing beyond the current BKC + IC + joint-commitment-formation evidence (e.g., if PM `pm:CommitmentBundle` formation in a future PM implementation becomes systematically PSM-primary rather than SR-primary; if Octo agent-architecture develops genuine dyadic PSM with collaborators), strengthening Q-b from partial to strong. Combined with Q-a protocol-surface emergence (R-0063-1), slug admission would become warranted.

- (R-0063-3) A dedicated PSM-to-Signal operationalization bridge emerges in the enactivist literature (e.g., Di Paolo et al. *Linguistic Bodies* successor work that specifies the interaction-process operations at institutional-scale; Gallagher *Enactivist Interventions* successor that bridges the programmatic treatment to operational protocols) that Spore's corpus review can cite as closing the Q-a thinness gap without requiring outside-cluster support.

- (R-0063-4) ADR-0050 Side-B relationship evolves: if future canon revisits whether `joint-commitment` and PSM should be given symmetric canonical status (both as primitives, or both as modes-of-joint-commitment-operation), ADR-0063's parallel-articulation prose would need revision. Trigger would be operator-directed Side-B re-engagement.

- (R-0063-5) A downstream-propagation audit surfaces that the dual-phase cases (federation protocol-version negotiation / PM matchmaking dialogue / spec-DAG amendment discussion) consistently tilt toward PSM-primary or SR-primary rather than exhibiting genuine dual-phase behavior, forcing a re-characterization of the dual-mode reading.

## Alternatives Considered

- **(a) Primitive admission.** Rejected: earning-test dual-passage fails (Q-a marginal + Q-b partial) and tradition-breadth block maintained (single full cluster + partial convergence across B/C/D/E; adjacent traditions cluster as family-claim supportive background, not as independent convergent arrivals at PSM-specific mechanism). Admitting PSM as 10th primitive would over-claim applicability to all signal-instances (false for algedonic / stigmergic / dissemination) and contradict ADR-0053 R-Sig-1's explicit single-tradition-support insufficiency framing.

- **(b) Cross-cutting doctrine.** Shape-mismatch: doctrine category is for visibility-lens + practice-orientation elements (reproductive-commoning / boundary-commoning / care-commoning per ADR-0002/0003/0045), not for cognitive-science framings of meaning-emergence. Structurally excluded on "for lenses, not for structurally-constitutive concerns" basis.

- **(c) Mode-across-primitives.** Shape-mismatch: modes category is for power-flow contrasts (allocational / expressive / constructed per ADR-0047/0048), not for meaning-making mechanisms. PSM is a sense-making-mode framing, not a power-flow mode.

- **(d) Property-on-Signal (or Field).** Rejected: per ADR-0062 precedent, joint-passage Q-b failure (PSM does not apply to all signal-instances) → scope-condition is the canonical choice over property. Property admission would imply universal applicability, which is empirically false. For Field: PSM could characterize deliberation-phase field-conditions, but Field's operations (set-conditions, scope-commitments, rule-level stratification per ADR-0046) are Ostromian-constitutive rather than PSM-framed.

- **(e) Derived glossary slug.** Rejected on tradition-breadth + over-engineering grounds: tradition-citation breadth is only marginally met for lighter admission (~2 cluster-equivalents); adding a slug without canon-body prose extension is minimally informative given PSM is already named multiple times in existing canon-body (project-vision.md:60 + :66 + :85; ADR-0050:141). Slug-without-scope carries vocabulary-governance risk of readers applying the slug universally to signal-instances where SR is operationally sufficient.

- **(f) Decline-inline-prose-only.** Rejected on lose-positive-articulation grounds: unlike ADR-0061's structural Q4 failure across all candidates, ADR-0063 faces genuine partial operational support with three strong PSM-supporting cases. Pure decline would lose the canonical articulation of dual-phase operational reality that the scope-conditioning preserves.

- **(g) Decompose-and-park-as-framing-note.** Rejected on primitive-bullet-anchoring grounds: the dual-phase finding lives at Signal specifically, and Signal-bullet scope-conditioning keeps the canonical home with the primitive rather than routing to a separate framing-note. ADR-0055's encounter-as-composition framing-note pattern fit the Encounter case because encounter was DECOMPOSABLE into existing primitives (Signal + Joint-commitment + Intent + Evidence within bounded Field-conditions); ADR-0063's case is different — PSM is not a composition but a sense-making-mode of the Signal primitive. Signal-bullet scope-conditioning is the per-precedent canonical choice (ADR-0062).

- **(h) Park-with-triggers.** Rejected on over-deferral grounds: PSM has been held-open since ADR-0053 R-Sig-1 (2026-04-22, one day before ADR-0063). ADR-0063 IS the dedicated evaluation that R-Sig-1's revisit-trigger named (*"Held-open per capstone §8 item 16"*); re-parking without disposition is a process failure. ADR-0054's park-with-triggers pattern fit the rewilding case because rewilding evidence was absent/speculative at decision time; ADR-0063's case is different — PSM has present operational evidence in at least three instance families and a dedicated bridge note + corpus review already in-repo.

- **(i.2) prose + slug admission.** Rejected on tradition-breadth + over-engineering grounds: tradition-citation breadth is only marginally met; slug addition would force yaml v12→v13 without clearing the single-tradition-support concern with full independence. The prose-only sub-option i.1 preserves canonical articulation without minting vocabulary on marginal evidence.

- **(i)+(g) combined: scope-condition + framing-note.** Rejected on heaviest-scope grounds: for a Signal-specific dual-mode finding, prose-only at Signal bullet is sufficient per ADR-0062 precedent. Cross-primitive relationship articulation (Signal + joint-commitment-formation + IC-memory-governance) is adequately handled inline in the Signal-bullet scope-conditioning and in the ADR §Decision + §Consequences prose. If future operator-review determines a separate framing-note is warranted, that pathway remains open as noted in §Consequences deferred.
