# ADR-0062 — Membrane-as-Self-Produced Disposition: Adopt-with-Scope-Conditioning (Option g)

## Status

draft (authored 2026-04-23 under `adr-0062-membrane-as-self-produced-disposition` decision-gated plan; Option (g) adopt-with-scope-conditioning + sub-options principled-rule + slug-inclusion=no/v12 per operator Step-2 decision-gate)

## Context

### Deferral source: ADR-0053 R-Mem-1 residue

ADR-0053 (glossary-refinements-bundled, Phase 3b.7, 2026-04-22) admitted three derived glossary slugs on Membrane: `attestation-of-execution` on Evidence, `permeability` on Membrane (Bollier-Helfrich semi-permeable framing), and `double-boundary` on Membrane (Ostrom Cox-1A/1B social-plus-ecological framing). ADR-0053 §R-Mem-1 (line 148) explicitly parked the autopoiesis-active reading of Membrane as canonically-acknowledged residue:

> **R-Mem-1 — Membrane-as-self-produced (autopoiesis-active reading).** Capstone §5 line 185 (3rd flagged confusion) notes that *"Canonical Layering treats boundary as passive (property of layers); Autopoiesis treats membrane as produced by the system it bounds (active); Ostrom treats boundary as double (social + ecological). Spore's membrane currently reads closer to the Canonical Layering view — if Spore takes autopoiesis seriously (which its field primitive implies), membrane should become produced-by-the-holon."* ADR-0053 admits `permeability` + `double-boundary` axes but does NOT make Membrane self-produced-by-holon. The autopoiesis-active reading is a separate canon-architectural decision not bundled into 3b.7's glossary scope. Operates as canonically-acknowledged residue; revisit triggers: dedicated Membrane-architectural ADR (post-Phase-3b) or Phase-2c canon work.

ADR-0062 is the dedicated Membrane-architectural ADR named in R-Mem-1's revisit trigger.

### Canon-state baseline at ADR authoring

- 9 primitives (3 structural: field / holon-irreducible-with-relational-identity-property / membrane + 6 verbs: intent / commitment / joint-commitment / evidence / signal / reproduction)

- 2 properties-on-primitives (holon-irreducibility, relational-identity — both on Holon per ADR-0050/0051)
- 6 derived glossary slugs (reciprocity, trust, attestation-of-execution, permeability, double-boundary, encounter)

- HEAD `7350f0d` post-ADR-0061 housekeeping

### Step 0.5 audit: eight operational cases

The earning-test evaluation enumerated eight per-instance-family Membrane operational cases and classified each as autopoiesis-active-supporting, passive-boundary-supporting, or ambiguous:

1. **BKC commons-membrane** (autopoiesis-active STRONG). The Bioregional Knowledge Commons membrane — governing who participates in BKC governance, which external data and entities cross into the commons, how knowledge is attributed — is constituted by ongoing commoning labor (stewardship rotations, ontology curation, attribution work, inclusion/exclusion governance). Bollier-Helfrich (via ADR-0053 `permeability` citation lineage) and Federici (via reproductive-commoning bridge note §framing) directly support the reading that the commons membrane IS the commoning activity, not a pre-declared boundary the activity fills in.

2. **IC memory-stewardship-membrane** (autopoiesis-active MODERATE). IC's memory-governance boundary (what enters the memory-layers, how attribution is maintained, which contributions persist) is produced by ongoing curation and attribution labor (per `intelligence-primitives.md` post-ic:ADR-0018 §Memory-Governance and Care-Commoning: *"attribution labor, curation labor, and preservation labor in an intelligence commons are asymmetric-care-relations between memory stewards and commons beneficiaries"*). The `autopoiesis-and-structural-coupling.md` bridge note (IC) supports the operational-closure/informational-openness distinction but explicitly caveats that IC layers have not been operationally tested as autopoietic-in-the-strong-sense.

3. **Octo agent-membrane** (ambiguous, tilts autopoiesis-active). Octo's operational boundary (what knowledge it accesses, what federation it participates in) is maintained by ongoing KOI-net federation protocol compliance and knowledge-gardening operations. Spore-documentation evidence is thin; federation-membership layer has declarative elements.

4. **Federation member-membership-membrane** (ambiguous, mixed). When a federation node joins the Spore/BKC/KOI-net federation, membership is sustained by ongoing protocol-compliance work but defined by declarative spec. `federation-protocol.md` L45 notes the four-boundary-theory-variant discipline: *"filtering-membrane (De Angelis), autopoietic closure (Maturana-Varela), pluriversal ontological difference (Escobar), cosmo-local subsidiarity (Ramos / Bauwens / Kostakis)"* — Spore does NOT commit to any single variant for federation membership.

5. **Federation protocol-version-membrane** (passive-boundary STRONG). A specific protocol-version's boundary — the compatibility scope that defines what it can interoperate with — is declared in the spec-DAG via `depends_on` edges. Per ADR-0041 text-authoritative discipline: text is the artifact; graph is derived. The protocol-version membrane exists as long as the text-authoritative spec exists.

6. **Governance artifact boundary** (passive-boundary STRONG). Governance artifacts (visions, roadmaps, agreements, policies, role definitions, domain definitions) define scope via text content. Per governance-artifacts-and-graph-projections.md L16: *"A governance artifact is any normative artifact that says 'this is what we intend.'"* The boundary is declared at authoring time.

7. **Spec-DAG node boundary** (passive-boundary STRONG). Each governed document has a boundary defined by frontmatter `depends_on` references and `doc_kind`. Per ADR-0041: text is authoritative; graph is derived. No ongoing labor required to maintain the boundary — it exists as long as the file exists with its frontmatter.

8. **PM `pm:CommitmentBundle` boundary** (ambiguous, tilts passive-boundary). A CommitmentBundle has a boundary — what intents it aggregates, what parties it binds — established at formation (`form-joint-commitment` operation per ADR-0050). The bundle's terms are maintained by ongoing attestation, but the boundary itself is declaratively established.

Aggregate classification: **2 autopoiesis-active-supporting / 3 passive-boundary-supporting / 3 ambiguous.**

### Tradition-citation evaluation

**Autopoiesis-active reading:**
- Maturana & Varela 1972 (primary) — an autopoietic system continuously regenerates the network that produced it, including its topological domain of realization. Membrane in this tradition IS the ongoing self-production.
- Bollier-Helfrich commons-as-self-produced (secondary) — already cited in ADR-0053 via `permeability` admission. The commons membrane is continuously produced by commoning activity.
- Thompson 4E enactivism (tertiary) — Varela-Thompson-Rosch 1991 extension; boundaries are enacted through coupling, not pre-declared. Weaker lineage for coordination-system application.

**Passive-boundary reading:**
- ADR-0041 text-authoritative representation (primary) — Spore's core discipline for governed artifacts: text is authoritative; graph is derived. Applies to spec-DAG nodes, governance artifacts, protocol-version specifications.
- Ostrom descriptive boundary analysis (secondary) — already admitted via ADR-0053 `double-boundary` slug. Ostrom's Cox-1A/1B analytical boundaries are identifiable independently of the labor that happens within them.

Both readings exceed the ≥2-tradition threshold per the plan's Step 0.5 discipline.

### Field-Membrane internal-consistency assessment

The capstone §5 line 185 hint (*"if Spore takes autopoiesis seriously (which its field primitive implies), membrane should become produced-by-the-holon"*) is real but **not load-bearing**. Field is autopoiesis-resonant in an Ostromian-constitutive sense per ADR-0046 (rules-in-use constitute action arenas; rule-in-use vs. rule-in-form maps to ADR-0041's text-authoritative-with-graph-derived discipline) rather than strictly in a Maturana-Varela autopoietic sense. Field is described in project-vision.md L27 as *"the shared coordination space; what is between holons; the ecological, economic, or epistemic substrate in which commitments travel. Field is agent-aware but not itself an agent"* — constitutively-defined by what occurs in it, but not self-produced-by-the-system-it-bounds in the strict autopoietic sense. Field and Membrane are distinct primitives with distinct production-modes; honest cross-primitive asymmetry is compatible with Spore's scoped-grammar stance (project-vision.md §Scope and frame acknowledgment: *"This is a scoped grammar, not a universal account of coordination"*).

### Earning-test verdict

**(Q-a) Protocol-surface specifiability**: MARGINAL. Proposed operations (`reproduce-membrane`, `maintain-boundary-through-labor`) are outcomes of existing doctrines (reproductive-commoning ADR-0002; care-commoning ADR-0045) rather than distinct protocol surfaces with independent governance. Unlike ADR-0049's Reproduction-continuity where "the thinness IS the invisibilization phenomenon" was a load-bearing Federicist argument because reproductive labor IS structurally invisible (and naming it creates protocol-surface for that invisible work), `membrane-as-self-produced` does not carry the same claim: the labor-visibility dimension is already canon-named at doctrine-lens level.

**(Q-b) Multi-scale operational implementations**: PARTIAL. Two genuine autopoiesis-active cases (BKC STRONG + IC MODERATE) + three clear passive-boundary counterexamples + three ambiguous cases. Does not meet the ADR-0049/0050 admission standard (four-plus strong cases) but also does not fail as structurally as ADR-0061 (0 admission-supporting / 4 ambiguous + Q4 structural failure across all candidates).

Joint-passage test (ADR-0044 lines 98-99: *"A primitive enters the grammar by passing two conditions jointly"*) NOT MET for slug or property admission. Per ADR-0048 parsimony-as-earning-test-outcome-not-axiom discipline, canon does not force admission — but also does not force pure decline when the operational reality is genuinely partial.

## Decision

**Option (g) adopt-with-scope-conditioning, with sub-options principled-rule + slug-inclusion=no/v12.**

Operator selected Option (g) at Step-2 decision-gate. Rationale: the Membrane primitive operationally spans both production-modes in current Spore usage; a property or slug admission would over-claim the autopoiesis-active reading's applicability to all membranes, and a pure decline would lose the positive articulation of genuine BKC/IC operational reality. Scope-conditioning prose at the primitive-bullet level is the honest description of a primitive whose operational instances span two distinct production-modes.

The scope-conditioning shape uses **principled-rule** (ongoing-commoning-labor-constituted vs. text-authoritative-declarative) rather than per-instance-family enumeration, with 2-3 parenthetical example instances per side. The principled-rule form keeps the precedent general enough to extend and avoids an ever-growing enumeration burden as new instantiations arise.

No slug admission: `membrane-as-self-produced` is NOT added to concepts-p2p-wiki.yaml. Concepts yaml holds at v12.

No property admission: the property-on-primitive count holds at 2 (holon-irreducibility + relational-identity, both on Holon per ADR-0050/0051).

No new canon-object-class: the four-category inventory (primitives / cross-cutting doctrines / modes-across-primitives / properties-on-primitives) is preserved. Scope-conditioning is prose at the primitive-bullet level, not a new canon-object-class.

### Method-precedent contribution

ADR-0062 establishes **primitive-bullet scope-conditioning** as a new canon-shift pattern, analogous to ADRs-0031/0032/0044 universality-overreach scope-conditioning at Core Thesis level but applied at primitive-bullet level. Pattern shape: when a concept's operational evidence applies to a scoped subset of a primitive's instances, extend the primitive bullet with explicit scope clauses (principled-rule preferred over enumerated-instance-family, with examples parenthetical to the rule) rather than forcing admit/decline.

The `decision: scope-condition` frontmatter value is novel with ADR-0062. Plan authoritative rule permitted `decision: edit` fallback if validator rejected the novel value; validator does not enforce specific `decision:` enumerations (confirmed at Step 5 verification), so `scope-condition` is retained as the canonically-descriptive value.

## Consequences

### Positive

- **R-Mem-1 residue closed.** ADR-0053's explicit parking of the autopoiesis-active reading is now resolved via scope-conditioning that honors both readings operationally. The capstone §5 line 185 three-reading framing (Canonical Layering / Autopoiesis-active / Ostrom-double) is now canon-legibly addressed: Canonical Layering = text-authoritative mode; Autopoiesis-active = self-produced mode; Ostrom-double = `double-boundary` slug (already admitted via ADR-0053, unchanged).

- **Operational pluralism preserved honestly.** Spore's Membrane primitive now carries explicit canon-body prose describing that different instantiations use different production-modes, avoiding the false choice between universal-autopoiesis and universal-text-authoritative framings.

- **Primitive-bullet scope-conditioning pattern established.** Future parked-candidate dispositions that fail the joint-passage earning test but carry genuine partial operational support can use this shape. Candidates currently in the parked-dedicated-ADR queue (participatory-sense-making admission per ADR-0053 R-Sig-1; co-presence-Field-condition sub-stratification per ADR-0055 R-Enc-1) can be evaluated against this pattern when their dedicated ADRs are authored.

- **Four canon-object-class inventory preserved.** No new category introduced; scope-conditioning prose uses existing canon-body machinery at the primitive-bullet level.

- **Concepts yaml stability.** yaml holds at v12. No slug admission burden; no vocabulary-governance risk of readers applying a universal slug to partial-operational cases.

- **Field-Membrane internal-consistency honestly addressed.** Capstone's suggestive parallelism ("if Field autopoiesis-resonant, Membrane should be autopoiesis-active") acknowledged but not forced. Cross-primitive asymmetry is explicit and defended.

- **Parsimony-as-earning-test-outcome discipline extended to partial-evidence case.** ADR-0048's discipline operated cleanly on clear pass (ADR-0049/0050 admissions) and clear fail (ADR-0054/0061 declines, ADR-0055 decompose-as-framing-note); ADR-0062 demonstrates that the same discipline handles partial-operational-evidence via scope-conditioning, a third canonically-honest outcome.

### Negative / Cost

- **New frontmatter value (`decision: scope-condition`).** Previously-used values across ADRs 0044-0061 are `admit`, `decline`, `park`, `edit`. Introducing `scope-condition` adds a fifth value. Validator does not enforce enumeration (observed at Step 5), so the cost is descriptive-vocabulary-addition, not a schema burden.

- **Primitive-bullet density increase.** The Membrane bullet in project-vision.md gains a fifth sub-bullet (after permeability / double-boundary / asymmetric authorization / permeability-and-double-boundary-are-derived-vocabulary). Readability risk modest given existing bullet density; condensed parallel in governance-artifacts-and-graph-projections.md keeps the parallel section from bloating at the same rate.

- **Scope-conditioning pattern requires discipline.** Principled-rule framing (rather than per-instance-family enumeration) is explicit in this ADR; future applications of the pattern will need to maintain the same discipline to avoid scope-conditioning degenerating into exhaustive case-lists.

### Neutral / Deferred

- **Downstream-propagation scope.** The ADR-0062 edits land in project-vision.md + governance-artifacts-and-graph-projections.md. Other canon-body files that discuss Membrane (federation-protocol.md §Boundary-Theory-Variant Discipline; research docs) are not modified by ADR-0062 but may benefit from scope-conditioning cross-reference during a future downstream-propagation audit (parallel to ADR-0059/0060 propagation pattern for prior semantic ADRs).

- **IC + PM alignment.** IC's `intelligence-primitives.md` and PM's `grammar.md` cite Spore Membrane canon by ADR number (per ic:ADR-0018 and pm:ADR-0014). The next IC/PM coordinated grammar-alignment phase can incorporate ADR-0062's scope-conditioning when it runs (downstream of ADR-0059/0060 audit). No same-session IC/PM canon edits are in scope for ADR-0062.

- **Slug-admission re-visit.** If future operational pressure surfaces — e.g., a distinct protocol surface emerges for self-produced membrane operations (rotation-of-stewardship-authority? commoning-pool-closure?) that is not captured by existing reproductive-commoning / care-commoning doctrines — the scope-conditioning can be upgraded to slug admission under a future ADR. ADR-0062 does not preclude that pathway; it records the current earning-test verdict.

- **Haraway sympoiesis critique.** Not directly addressed by ADR-0062. Haraway's critique (*"nothing makes itself; nothing is really autopoietic or self-organizing"*) challenges the autopoiesis tradition's self-production framing. Spore's scope-conditioning accommodates the critique partially: self-produced-membranes are explicitly described as constituted by *commoning / stewardship / care labor* (i.e., labor of persons-within-the-commons, not self-organizing without labor), which is closer to Haraway's sympoietic "making-with" than to the stronger self-producing-alone claim. A more direct engagement with Haraway's critique is deferred to potential future research-docs work if convergence-evidence warrants.

### Re-visit conditions

ADR-0062 records a scope-conditioning verdict. It does not close the concept permanently. Canon may revisit if:

- (R-0062-1) An operational case emerges that is unambiguously autopoiesis-active AND exhibits a distinct protocol surface (operations not decomposable into existing doctrines' operations). Promotion to derived glossary slug would then be warranted under ADR-0044 earning-test Q-a pass.

- (R-0062-2) Three or more new operational cases emerge that are unambiguously autopoiesis-active across Spore's instance families (beyond BKC/IC/Octo current evidence), strengthening Q-b from partial to strong. Combined with a Q-a protocol-surface emergence (R-0062-1), admission would become the correct disposition.

- (R-0062-3) A future ADR on Field or Holon primitives makes Field strictly autopoietic rather than Ostromian-constitutive, forcing Field-Membrane symmetry as a canonical commitment. Scope-conditioning on Membrane would then require revision.

- (R-0062-4) Haraway sympoiesis critique becomes load-bearing for Spore canon via convergence-evidence (≥3 bridge notes, ≥2 traditions) — scope-conditioning would need to re-characterize the self-produced mode as sympoietic-making-with rather than autopoietic-self-producing.

## Alternatives Considered

- **(a) Admit as named property-on-Membrane.** Rejected: over-claims operational reality (implies all Spore membranes are self-produced, which is false for spec-DAG / governance-artifact / protocol-version cases). Earning-test Q-a MARGINAL + Q-b PARTIAL does not meet joint-passage standard.

- **(b) Mode-across-primitives.** Structurally excluded per plan: modes are qualities of operation per ADR-0048 (allocational / expressive / constructed power), not production-modes of structural primitives. Self-produced-vs-text-authoritative is a production-mode distinction, not an operational-quality distinction.

- **(c) Derived glossary slug alongside permeability/double-boundary.** Rejected: a slug admission without scope-conditioning creates vocabulary-governance risk (readers may apply the slug universally). While the slug's one-line definition could carry scope, the slug itself then becomes an unusual construct (a scoped slug, unlike `permeability` / `double-boundary` which apply universally as analytic axes).

- **(d) Decline-inline-prose-only per ADR-0061.** Rejected: unlike ADR-0061's structural Q4 failure across all candidates, ADR-0062 faces genuine partial operational support. Pure decline would lose the positive articulation of BKC/IC autopoiesis-active reality that the scope-conditioning preserves.

- **(e) Decompose-and-park-as-framing-note per ADR-0055.** Rejected on primitive-bullet-visibility grounds: a framing-note is less canon-visible than primitive-bullet prose, and the operational split is load-bearing enough to warrant bullet-level acknowledgment. ADR-0055's encounter framing-note pattern fit the encounter-as-composition case because encounter was DECOMPOSABLE into existing primitives; ADR-0062's case is different — Membrane already exists as a primitive, and what needs articulation is not a composition but a production-mode scope.

- **(f) Park-with-triggers per ADR-0054.** Rejected on evidence-is-present-now grounds: BKC + IC operational cases are operational today, not speculative-future. ADR-0054's rewilding-decline-with-triggers pattern fit the rewilding case because rewilding evidence was absent/speculative at decision time; ADR-0062's case is different — autopoiesis-active reading has present operational evidence in at least two instance families.
