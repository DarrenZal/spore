# ADR-0047 — Power as Multi-Layer Decomposition (rule-setting authority + asymmetric-Commitment + asymmetric-Membrane)

## Status

active (authored + activated 2026-04-22 under `canon-rebuild-phase-3b-3-power-authority-disposition` plan, Option G.full)

## Context

The Phase 1 coherence-and-falsifiability audit (`tmp/canon-coherence-falsifiability-audit-2026-04-22.md`) and the corpus-foundational-review-v1 capstone (`docs/research/corpus-review/research-capstone.md`) both surfaced Power/Authority as a Phase 3b-deferred item. Phase 3 (ADR-0044) enumerated Power alongside Care and Norms in the Phase 3b deferral list. Phase 3b.1 (ADR-0045) closed Care via Option F (third cross-cutting doctrine). Phase 3b.2 (ADR-0046) closed Norms via Option B (Field rule-level stratification). Phase 3b.3 closes Power/Authority.

The capstone §3 Power/Authority verdict is authoritative:

> *"Admit as primitive OR as named asymmetry on commitment/membrane. Second-sharpest gap [after Care]."*
> — `docs/research/corpus-review/research-capstone.md` §3

The capstone §3 paraphrase-test is explicit about single-primitive-asymmetry failure:

> *"'Capacity to bind others' commitments' captures VSM but misses asymmetric-vulnerability and enumerated-powers patterns. Spore's symmetric holons reproduce the Baier/Jackson/Federici critique."*
> — `docs/research/corpus-review/research-capstone.md` §3

Power is structurally unlike Care and Norms. Tradition-count is thinner (5 load-bearing + 2 load-bearing-as-critique, vs. 8 for Care, 10 for Norms), but the absence is itself a tradition-level finding: Philosophy of Collective Agency is *"peculiarly silent"*; REA/ValueFlows is *"structurally absent"*; Autopoiesis has *"no systematic enactive treatment"*; MARL is *"symmetric by default"*. Where Power IS load-bearing, it appears in **four distinct structural modes**:

- **(a) enumerated-powers-by-role** — Debian §4/§5/§6; PEP 13 Steering Council; Apache PMC/Board distinction; Searle's deontic-powers stratifier.
- **(b) asymmetric-vulnerability** — Baier 1986 "Trust and Antitrust"; Folbre on patriarchal power in care-relations; Kittay "nested dependencies"; Tronto four-phase care-asymmetry.
- **(c) power-capture critique** — Federici/De Angelis/Caffentzis on commons co-option by capital; Kostakis two-layer admin-class-accumulation.
- **(d) relational-spiritual authority** — Borrows' five-sources-of-law (sacred/natural/deliberative/customary/positivistic); authority via ancestors, land, elder-consensus, ritual.

No single dominant formalization exists (unlike Norms' Ostrom 7-rule-types or Care's Tronto four-phase). Any single-primitive-asymmetry admission captures at most one mode and leaves the other three uncaptured — the capstone-explicit paraphrase-test failure.

Eight disposition options were enumerated in the Phase 3b.3 decision-brief (`tmp/canon-phase3b3-power-disposition-decision-brief-2026-04-22.md`):

- **Option A** — Power as 8th primitive (A.1 verb / A.2 new primitive-kind / A.3 asymmetric-agency): rejected — breaks 7-primitive parsimony for a tradition-count (5) thinner than Care (8) and Norms (10) which held parsimony against them.
- **Option B** — Power as named asymmetric-Commitment variant: rejected alone — capstone §3 paraphrase-test explicitly names this failure.
- **Option C** — Power as named asymmetric-Membrane variant: rejected alone — partial-pass on gatekeeping only.
- **Option D** — Power as authority-over-rule-levels (Phase-3b.2-enabled; novel post-capstone option): rejected alone — captures enumerated-powers + polycentricity + VSM stratification, but misses asymmetric-vulnerability (Care/Folbre/Kittay) and relational-spiritual authority (Pluriversal).
- **Option E** — Power as pattern-library entry: rejected — proportion-mismatch to "second-sharpest gap" stakes; same insufficiency pattern Phase 3b.1 rejected for Care and Phase 3b.2 rejected for Norms.
- **Option F** — Power as fourth cross-cutting doctrine (`power-commoning`): rejected — category-fit marginal; doesn't land §3 limbs.
- **Option G.full** — Power decomposed across three structural layers (rule-setting authority + asymmetric-binding + asymmetric-gatekeeping) with synthesizing "Power across primitives" paragraph: **selected**.
- **Option H** — Power as critique-lens extending ADR-0005: rejected alone — doesn't land §3 limbs; mostly restates ADR-0005.

Operator rationale for G.full over D-alone centered on honest capstone §3 compliance: Option D addresses only the enumerated-powers limb; asymmetric-vulnerability (Baier/Jackson/Folbre/Kittay) is structurally uncaptured by D. G.full's three-layer decomposition addresses BOTH limbs — Layer-1 captures enumerated-powers; Layer-2 captures asymmetric-vulnerability. Three layers articulate rather than invisibilise (distinct from Care Option C / Norms Option C invisibilisation failure) because each retains first-class visibility at its own primitive surface.

## Decision

**edit.** Canon admits Power/Authority as **multi-layer structural decomposition** across three existing primitives, with a synthesizing frame held at grammar level. No new primitive is coined; 7-primitive parsimony preserved; all three layers extend existing primitives' asymmetry axes.

**Layer 1 — Rule-setting authority as Field-rule-level property** (built atop ADR-0046):

The Field primitive's 3-level rule-stratification (operational/collective-choice/constitutional, per ADR-0046) carries a power dimension: capacity-to-set, capacity-to-amend, and capacity-to-enforce rules at each level is distributed across participants, roles, and positions. This is the enumerated-powers pattern from the Governance-Process tradition (Debian §4/§5/§6 enumerated powers by role; PEP 13 Steering Council powers; Apache PMC vs Board distinction) and the polycentric-governance pattern (V. Ostrom 1972; Aligica-Tarko 2012 — multiple centers with overlapping authority at different rule-levels). VSM's S5/S3 stratification (Beer, *Heart of Enterprise* ch. 14: "enough authority to maintain cohesion, but not so much as to destroy the autonomy of its viable components") maps here. The canon slug `authority-over-rule-levels` names this power-axis; the ADR-0046 rule-level stack provides the substrate.

**Layer 2 — Asymmetric binding as named variant of Commitment:**

Commitment-as-primitive has a symmetric default reading (Gilbert's joint-commitment, Bratman's interlocking plans) that external critics (Baier 1986, Held, Kittay, Folbre) have named as the tradition's characteristic blind-spot. Baier: *"contract/commitment-centric moral theory treats relations as simply cases of comembership in a kingdom of ends, in the given temporary conditions of one-sided dependence, ignoring the asymmetric vulnerability that characterizes trust."* Canon admits `asymmetric-commitment` as a named variant of Commitment where one party binds disproportionately (caregiver→dependent, employer→employee, state→subject, ruler→ruled, ancestor→descendant in Pluriversal sacred-commitment framings). The canon slug `asymmetric-commitment` names this binding-axis; this Layer captures the asymmetric-vulnerability mode that capstone §3 explicitly named as paraphrase-test failure for any single-primitive response.

Care-commoning (ADR-0045) operates as a cross-cutting doctrine lens over Commitment; `asymmetric-commitment` operates as the structural-party-asymmetry axis of Commitment. They are distinguishable: Care-commoning makes-visible-the-asymmetric-care-relation-pattern (disposition-and-practice applied-over-primitives); asymmetric-commitment names-the-binding-asymmetry-as-party-structure (constitutive asymmetry of the commitment's party composition). Care-commoning lenses over the machinery; the machinery is the `asymmetric-commitment` variant.

**Layer 3 — Asymmetric gatekeeping as named variant of Membrane:**

Membrane's existing authorize function (filter / translate / authorize / attest per `project-vision.md` Core Thesis Membrane bullet) carries a power dimension when authorize-capacity is distributed asymmetrically: some parties can admit/exclude/gate; others cannot. This is the gatekeeper-role-accrual pattern (ADR-0005 power-capture mechanism 3; Kostakis two-layer admin-capture), platform-gatekeeping, immigration-control-style boundary authority. The canon slug `asymmetric-membrane` names this authorization-asymmetry axis. Pluriversal membrane-authorization via ritual/elder-consensus maps to this Layer with an asymmetric-source (sacred/ancestral) rather than asymmetric-accumulation reading.

ADR-0005's administrator-capture declination names the failure mode at bundle-level; Layer-3 `asymmetric-membrane` names the structural capacity at primitive-level. Canon now has both: ADR-0005 is the critique-frame, ADR-0047 Layer-3 is the structural substrate administrator-capture operates through.

**Synthesizing frame — "Power across primitives":**

A dedicated paragraph in `project-vision.md` Core Thesis and parallel in `foundations/constitutional-artifacts-and-graph-projections.md` §Coordination Ecology names the three-layer structure explicitly, preventing the multi-layer decomposition from drifting to invisibilisation. The synthesizing frame makes clear that Power is not reducible to any single layer — enumerated-powers, asymmetric-vulnerability, and asymmetric-gatekeeping are distinct structural modes that canon expresses at distinct primitive surfaces. Power-capture (Federici/De Angelis/Caffentzis) is the compound-pattern where all three layers are captured simultaneously; ADR-0005's bundle-declination and ADR-0047's multi-layer primitives together let canon name both the critique-frame and its structural substrate.

**Pluriversal held-tension (ADR-0001 held):**

Borrows' five-sources-of-law (sacred / natural / deliberative / customary / positivistic) is irreducibly about *what can authorize*, not about *authority operating on rule-levels*. Layer-2 `asymmetric-commitment` accommodates sacred-commitment (asymmetric binding with ancestor/land/spirit) at mechanism-level; Layer-3 `asymmetric-membrane` accommodates ritual/elder-consensus authorization (asymmetric authorization via sacred source) at mechanism-level. Layer-1 `authority-over-rule-levels` carries a Western-legal-frame adjacency that conflicts with Borrows' ontological plurality — held under ADR-0001 as scoped-grammar-frame-acknowledgement, not resolved.

## Consequences

**Grammar-level:**
- 7-primitive roster preserved (ADR-0044). No new primitives.
- Cross-cutting doctrine category remains at 3 members (reproductive-commoning, boundary-commoning, care-commoning). Power-commoning/authority-commoning NOT admitted — Option F category-fit rejected.
- Three named variants admitted at primitive-asymmetry axis: `authority-over-rule-levels` (Field-rule-level power-axis), `asymmetric-commitment` (Commitment-party-asymmetry), `asymmetric-membrane` (Membrane-authorize-asymmetry).
- Synthesizing frame "Power across primitives" added to Core Thesis (project-vision.md) and mirrored in constitutional-artifacts-and-graph-projections.md §Coordination Ecology.

**Paraphrase-test verdict:** **partial-pass across all four modes.**

- (a) enumerated-powers: **passes** via Layer-1 authority-over-rule-levels.
- (b) asymmetric-vulnerability: **passes** via Layer-2 asymmetric-commitment.
- (c) power-capture critique: **passes via ADR-0005 bundle-declination + ADR-0047 multi-layer substrate composition**; ADR-0047 alone does not capture the compound-capture pattern, but the composition (ADR-0005 frame + ADR-0047 substrate) expresses it.
- (d) relational-spiritual authority: **partial-pass** via Layer-2 (sacred-commitment as asymmetric-binding with ancestor/land) and Layer-3 (sacred-membrane as asymmetric-authorization via ritual/elder-consensus); Layer-1 carries Western-legal-frame adjacency held under ADR-0001.

This is the first Phase-3b ADR to explicitly satisfy the full paraphrase-test across multiple modes rather than partial-pass on one. Partial-pass is acceptable on mode (d) given ADR-0001 held-tension discipline; full-pass would require abandoning the Ostrom-3-level framework or demoting ADR-0046, which Option G.full explicitly preserves.

**Cross-ADR relationships:**

- **ADR-0005 (decentralization-myth-bundle)**: complement + extend. ADR-0005 file unchanged. ADR-0005 names the critique-frame at bundle-declination level (three sub-critiques: decentralization-theater, digital-labor-as-free-gift, administrator-capture); ADR-0047 supplies the primitive-level structural substrate that administrator-capture operates through (Layer-3 asymmetric-membrane = gatekeeper-role-accrual as named mechanism). Future edits invoking power-capture critique can compose: ADR-0005 for the frame, ADR-0047 for the structural substrate.
- **ADR-0044 (core-thesis-primitive-roster-alignment)**: preserved. 7-primitive count intact; three asymmetry variants extend existing primitives, not admit new ones.
- **ADR-0045 (care-cross-cutting-doctrine)**: interact-required-distinction. Care-commoning doctrine-lens (disposition-and-practice applied-over-primitives) is orthogonal to Power Layer-2 `asymmetric-commitment` structural variant (constitutive party-asymmetry). Care-commoning lenses over asymmetric-commitment instances that bear care-relations; asymmetric-commitment is broader (includes power-asymmetric bindings that are not care-relations: ruler/ruled, employer/employee, contractual asymmetry). Prose in Core Thesis must distinguish explicitly to prevent conflation. ADR-0045 file unchanged.
- **ADR-0046 (field-rule-level-stratification)**: directly-built-atop. Layer-1 `authority-over-rule-levels` IS authority-over-ADR-0046-stratification. Phase-3b.2 ADR-0046 §Consequences explicitly flagged this framing as natural. ADR-0046 §Consequences paragraph "authority-to-set/amend-rules-at-level is natural framing over the stratification; enumerated-powers pattern canon-expressible; polycentric-governance gains structural machinery" is realized by ADR-0047 Layer-1.
- **ADR-0001 (pluriversal-incommensurability, held)**: held-tension-extends. Layer-1's Western-legal-frame adjacency increases held-tension surface on ADR-0001; prose acknowledgment required. Layer-2 and Layer-3 accommodate relational-spiritual authority at mechanism-level without resolving ontological-plurality.
- **ADR-0016 (field-holon-distinction)**: preserved. Power distribution operates within fields and across holonic boundaries; the field/holon distinction is not affected.
- **ADR-0041 (text-authoritative-representation)**: preserved. Power-at-primitive-layer is canon text; graph projections of power-asymmetries derive from canon and supplementary frontmatter.

**Phase 3b remainder implications:**

- **Phase 3b.4 (Reproduction-as-field-property)**: Layer-1 authority-over-rule-levels means reproduction-continuity-power is expressible via authority-over-reproduction-rules. Reduces Phase 3b.4 intersection-cost.
- **Phase 3b.5 (Identity-as-holon-property)**: Layer-3 `asymmetric-membrane` maps cleanly to identity-as-entry-rule-asymmetry.
- **Phase 3b.6 (Reciprocity + Trust bundle)**: Layer-2 `asymmetric-commitment` directly expresses reciprocity-asymmetric; Apache "earned authority" trust-framework maps to Layer-1 authority-over-rule-levels.
- **Phase 3b.7 (glossary refinements)**: orthogonal.

**Articulation-not-invisibilisation discipline:**

Option G.full escapes the Care-Option-C / Norms-Option-C invisibilisation failure mode because:
1. Each of the three layers retains first-class visibility at its own primitive surface (Field bullet, Commitment bullet, Membrane bullet in Core Thesis).
2. A dedicated synthesizing "Power across primitives" paragraph in Core Thesis and mirrored in §Coordination Ecology names the three-layer structure explicitly.
3. Three new concept slugs (`authority-over-rule-levels`, `asymmetric-commitment`, `asymmetric-membrane`) make the asymmetry axes first-class in vocab-governance rather than buried in prose.
4. Cross-ADR prose distinguishes Care-commoning-lens from Power-asymmetric-Commitment-machinery, preventing Care-Power conflation.
5. ADR-0005's critique-frame composes with ADR-0047's structural substrate, letting power-capture be canon-readable both as frame and as mechanism.

Without (1)-(4), three-layer decomposition would drift to invisibilisation — the operator's Decision-log rationale explicitly commits to the articulation discipline.
