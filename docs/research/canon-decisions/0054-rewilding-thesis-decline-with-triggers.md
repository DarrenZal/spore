# ADR-0054 — Rewilding-Thesis: Decline-with-Triggers (Phase 3b.8 Parking Formalization)

## Context

Phases 3b.1–3b.7 closed 2026-04-22 across 10 ADRs (0044–0053); canon reached its current state of 9 primitives + 3 cross-cutting doctrines + 2 modes-across-primitives + 2 Holon properties + 5 derived-glossary slugs (yaml v11). Phase 3b.8 disposes a candidate parked from Phase 3b.3b. ADR-0048 §Phase-3b.8-parking (lines 146-148) named Johar's *"Power Is Rewilding"* (2026) as a candidate for future consideration under a maturation condition, and ADR-0048 §Parking lot (line 184) reiterated it. The supporting synthesis at `tmp/johar-power-framework-synthesis-2026-04-22.md` GAP 4 (lines 141-148, 178, 188, 196) framed the same thesis as a **meta-gap** and classified it as "speculative enough that it should be deferred — future Phase item, not retroactive ADR-0047 edit" (line 178).

### The rewilding thesis

Johar's thesis argues that four historically-dependent conditions that once contained institutional power are decoupling: (1) concentration of force (state monopoly on legitimate violence); (2) mediation of injustice (courts, tribunals, ombuds channels); (3) agreement production (parliamentary and deliberative procedures); (4) shared legibility (common referential frames). As these decouple, Johar claims: *"Power is no longer reliably held within the architectures that once stabilized it, and is increasingly expressed across the field rather than through it."* Power returns to the field — distributed, ambient, continuous, situational.

### Why this is a meta-gap, not a primitive gap

ADR-0047 decomposes Power across three structural-asymmetry axes on existing primitives: authority-over-rule-levels on Field (Layer 1); asymmetric-commitment on Commitment (Layer 2); asymmetric-membrane on Membrane (Layer 3). ADR-0048 extends the treatment with two further modes-across-primitives — expressive-power and constructed-power — that together cover all four power-modes from capstone §3 (allocational / enumerated / asymmetric-vulnerability / relational-spiritual) plus Johar's three-form framework (allocational / expressive / constructed).

All three Power-layers are institutional framings: authority-over-rule-levels presupposes an institutional stratification (ADR-0046 Ostrom rule-levels); asymmetric-commitment presupposes protocol machinery that makes a commitment binding; asymmetric-membrane presupposes boundary-production that is institutionally stable enough to gatekeep. The rewilding thesis does not identify a missing layer or a missing mode; it questions whether the foundational assumption (power can be held within institutional structures) continues to hold as the four historically-dependent conditions decouple.

This is a meta-gap about **grammar-design-scope-conditions**: under what conditions is Spore's grammar sufficient to coordinate, and under what conditions would it prove insufficient? A missing primitive would be a concrete grammar-extension; a meta-gap is a concern about the grammar's domain of applicability. The distinction is load-bearing for disposition-shape: the earning-test (ADR-0048 parsimony-as-outcome-not-axiom) applies to primitive-admission candidates, not to meta-gap candidates.

## Decision

**Decline Phase 3b.8 canon-admission under current evidence. Formalize the parking-posture at ADR layer with an explicit, operationally-named re-opening trigger list.**

### What this ADR does

- **Records the disposition verdict.** ADR-0054 is the canonical record that Phase 3b.8 was evaluated and declined under current evidence. The parking-state previously lived distributed across ADR-0048 §Phase-3b.8-parking and the synthesis GAP 4 file; this ADR consolidates it at the ADR layer where future cycles expect to find disposition history.
- **Canonizes six re-opening triggers** (see §Maturation triggers below). Each trigger is operationally falsifiable — a future reviewer can check whether the trigger has fired without interpreting loose aesthetics.
- **Preserves canon object-class inventory unchanged.** No new primitives / doctrines / modes / properties / derived-glossary slugs. The 9-primitive / 3-doctrine / 2-mode / 2-property / 5-derived-glossary-slug roster is preserved exactly as ADR-0053 left it.

### What this ADR does NOT do

- **Does NOT foreclose future consideration.** The decline is a decline of canon-admission-now, not of canon-admission-ever. Trigger 4 (new Johar bridge-note with non-accommodable examples) is specifically scoped to preserve the path for source-scholarship maturation.
- **Does NOT admit new canon-object-classes.** Specifically, does NOT introduce a "meta-gaps" or "grammar-scope-conditions" 5th canon-object-class (Option D from decision-brief rejected on parsimony-discipline grounds — introducing new canon-object-class for a single speculative candidate inverts the earning-test pattern established in 3b.3b/3b.4/3b.5a).
- **Does NOT elevate rewilding-thesis to held-tension** per ADR-0001 pattern (Option C rejected on tension-shape-mismatch: ADR-0001 tension is genuinely-incommensurable-in-principle (ontological-pluriversality); rewilding tension is speculative-pending-maturation-evidence — different shape per ADR-0050 Option D rejection reasoning).
- **Does NOT admit rewilding-thesis as pattern-library entry** (Option B rejected on parking-verdict-honoring grounds — pattern-library admission without operational evidence is aspirational/speculative design-criteria that parking-verdict "not canonized now" prose excludes).
- **Does NOT bundle four-enabling-conditions pattern-library candidate** (ADR-0048 lines 150-152). That candidate remains parked for post-Phase-3b pattern-library work, independent of rewilding-thesis disposition.
- **Does NOT modify ADR-0047 or ADR-0048** retroactively. Both remain structurally intact; this ADR operates at meta-canon layer documenting the Phase 3b.8 disposition.
- **Does NOT close Phase 3b arc at primitive-admission layer.** Phase 3b arc was already closed at glossary level for the 9-primitive roster per ADR-0053. ADR-0054 closes the Phase 3b.8 disposition-slot, not the whole arc; Phase 3b arc is now fully disposed across all parked candidates (Phase 3b.8 declined; Encounter-as-10th-primitive remains on separate queue per operator routing).

## Maturation triggers

The following six triggers canonize the re-opening conditions under which Phase 3b.8 (or a successor phase) should re-evaluate the rewilding-thesis disposition. The list is deliberately maximal: the decline is of canon-admission-now, not of future consideration. Each trigger is operationally falsifiable — a future reviewer can check whether the trigger has fired against the named evidence-class without reliance on loose aesthetic judgment.

### Primary triggers (operational evidence of structural decomposition)

**Trigger 1 — Federation-node institutional collapse.** An instance-family member (Octo / Dobby / BKC / PM / DW / other Spore-instance) experiences *sustained* institutional-structure failure — distinct from technical outage, key rotation, staffing gap, or temporary governance-process disruption — where the institutional frame no longer reliably holds, and Spore's grammar (as of the ADR-0054-era canon state) proves insufficient to coordinate through the collapse. "Sustained" means persistent across more than one coordination cycle; "institutional-structure failure" means the authority-over-rule-levels machinery from ADR-0047 Layer 1 stops transmitting. Evidence-class: field-report documenting the collapse + demonstrated grammar-insufficiency.

**Trigger 2 — Sustained ambient-power phenomena in instance-family operations.** Field-reports (bridge-notes / meeting-notes / evidence-captures) document power flowing continuously across a field rather than through institutional layers, AND this ambient-power flow is *not* adequately captured by ADR-0047 three-layer decomposition or ADR-0048 expressive-power / constructed-power modes. "Sustained" means the phenomenon recurs across multiple independent observations; "not adequately captured" means analysts attempting to use the ADR-0047+0048 vocabulary find the phenomenon systematically escapes their framework. Evidence-class: 3+ independent bridge-notes or evidence-captures documenting the inadequacy.

### Internal-canon trigger

**Trigger 3 — Expressive / constructed-mode systematic collapse** (per ADR-0048 §Honest-limit-acknowledgment lines 154-156). If operational evidence shows Spore's existing primitives *systematically* collapsing expressive or constructed power into allocational framings — e.g., dissent reduced to opposition-signal with no renegotiation-affordance; improvisation blocked by over-determined interface design; pre-reaction collapsed to signal-with-reaction only — a dedicated amendment ADR may be required. ADR-0048 explicitly names this as an amendment trigger. "Systematic" means the collapse pattern repeats across independent instance-families, not a single-instance design bug. Evidence-class: pattern-documentation across 2+ instance-families.

### Source-scholarship trigger

**Trigger 4 — New Johar bridge-note on rewilding thesis with non-accommodable examples.** A new or expanded `johar-power-is-rewilding.md` (or equivalent) bridge-note in `docs/research/connections/` presents *operational examples* that Spore's grammar cannot accommodate, going beyond the current three-form-framework treatment in `johar-power-cannot-be-allocated.md`. The load-bearing gate is *non-accommodable examples*, not mere repetition of the speculative thesis itself. "Non-accommodable" means attempts to apply ADR-0047+0048 vocabulary to the example systematically fail to make operational sense of the power-dynamic. Evidence-class: bridge-note with specific operational examples + demonstrated grammar-failure on those examples.

### Cross-tradition convergence trigger

**Trigger 5 — Convergence of 3+ independent learning-field sources** (non-Johar) on institutional-decomposition / ambient-power / post-institutional-coordination as a *load-bearing design problem* for coordination-grammar work. The single-source-trap guard: single-tradition support (Johar only) is insufficient per the ADR-0048 parsimony-as-earning-test discipline extended to meta-gap evaluation. Three independent sources provide cross-tradition corroboration that the concern is structural rather than idiosyncratic. "Load-bearing design problem" means the sources treat institutional-decomposition as a concern that must shape coordination-grammar design, not merely describe it as a phenomenon. Evidence-class: 3+ bridge-notes or capstone citations from distinct non-Johar traditions.

### Procedural trigger

**Trigger 6 — Phase 2c / canon-review v3 cycle exogenous Tier-1 finding.** The meta-gap is surfaced as a Tier-1 finding by an exogenous review cycle (Phase 2c queue or canon-review v3 per learning-membrane frame-lock discipline — endogenous review cannot challenge the foundational frame it operates within). "Exogenous" means the review is conducted from outside the frame-lock (canon-review v3 per protocol triggers, or an external-collaborator foundational-review). Evidence-class: review-cycle finding-document with explicit Tier-1 classification.

### Trigger discipline

Any *one* trigger firing is sufficient to open a Phase 3b.8-v2 disposition cycle; the disposition cycle itself then evaluates all available evidence, not only the triggering evidence-class. Triggers are gates on re-opening, not gates on canon-admission — a re-opened cycle may still decline, admit at pattern-library layer, admit at held-tension layer, or admit at full canon-integration layer per the evidence weight at the time of re-evaluation.
