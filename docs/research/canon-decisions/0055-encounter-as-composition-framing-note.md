# ADR-0055 — Encounter: Admit as Derived-Glossary-Slug + Framing-Note + Pattern-Library Trigger (Option D + E.1 hybrid)

## Context

Encounter has been parked as a dedicated-ADR candidate since ADR-0048 line 73 (2026-04-22 earlier same day): *"Encounter (federation events generating constructed capacity): plausibly passes both tests; flagged as future primitive-admission candidate deserving dedicated ADR."* ADR-0050 line 231 recontextualized the candidate after the Phase 3b.4 Reproduction admission and Phase 3b.5a joint-commitment admission: *"Encounter-as-9th-primitive candidate (parked from 3b.3b earning-test, recontextualized after 3b.4) now becomes Encounter-as-10th-primitive candidate; dedicated ADR still required if operational pressure warrants."*

Phase 3b arc closed 2026-04-22 through ADR-0054 (rewilding-thesis-decline-with-triggers) at glossary-level for the 9-primitive roster. ADR-0055 is post-phase-3b work on the separately-queued Encounter candidate; it does not re-open Phase 3b or disturb any of its closures.

### The Encounter candidate

**Encounter** names *federation-event-generating-constructed-capacity* — the coordination-event where multiple holons co-present in a shared moment, producing situationally-emergent agency that was not pre-allocated by any structure. Instance-family examples Spore canon has gestured at:

- BKC/Octo quarterly federation meetings (Salish Sea cross-node gatherings)
- Poietic Match match-events (when mutual-match activation happens)
- DW stand-ups and design-reviews (cross-person encounter under shared focus)
- Cross-federation events (Octo-BKC quarterly; Spore × Jeff-Emmett-shop compose-events)
- Protocol-version-adoption moments (already covered by `joint-commitment` per ADR-0050)

### Step 0.5 honest earning-test evaluation

Full verification at `tmp/encounter-earning-test-verification.txt`. Summary:

**Test (a) — separable protocol surface.** Four candidate operations evaluated per-operation:

| Operation | Irreducibility finding |
|-----------|------------------------|
| convene (summon-participants to scheduled co-presence) | NO — Signal (invitation) + Joint-commitment (attendance-pledge) + Field-condition (scheduled window as rule-in-use per ADR-0046) |
| co-presence-establish (common-knowledge of mutual attention) | PARTIAL — common-knowledge structure (Lewis/Schiffer) is genuinely distinct from verb-compositions, but is **substrate-like**: a Field-condition in which verbs fire, not a coordination **act** performed by agents |
| encounter-state-emerge (shared-situation constructs) | NO — already named as **constructed-power mode** in ADR-0048; admitting at primitive-level would reopen ADR-0048 |
| encounter-close (participants disperse with state-changes) | NO — Commitment-attestation + Evidence-capture + Signal-transmission on dispersal |

**Verdict at verb-primitive level: (a) FAILS.** No candidate operation is a coordination-verb not covered by existing primitives plus Field-conditions.

**Verdict at substrate-sub-stratification level: (a) PARTIAL.** Co-presence-establish's common-knowledge-of-mutual-attention is substrate-like; a future extension of Field (parallel to ADR-0046 rule-level-stratification) could host it. This would not be a 10th primitive; it would extend Field, and the ADR-0055 disposition does not make that move — flags as parking-candidate only (see Parking lot).

**Test (b) — multi-scale operational pressure.** Four instance families evaluated:

| Instance family | Pressure | Primitive-uniqueness |
|-----------------|----------|---------------------|
| BKC/Octo bioregional stewardship | Strong (federation meetings, cross-node composition) | Federation-protocol-adoption already absorbed by `joint-commitment` (ADR-0050) |
| Poietic Match pair-matching | Real (match-events) | Match-event reads as outcome of Intent+Signal+Joint-commitment structured by Field-conditions |
| DW personal-operator | Real (stand-ups, design-reviews) | Conventionally described as patterns, not primitives |
| Cross-federation | Real (Octo-BKC quarterly, Spore × Jeff-Emmett) | Not primitive-unique |

**Verdict: (b) PASSES — but pressure is not primitive-unique.** The same pressure-pattern is equally consistent with pattern-library admission, Field-sub-stratification, or framing-note decomposition.

### Plan-vs-evidence catch (3b.4 discipline)

ADR-0048 line 73's *"plausibly passes both tests"* claim is **not confirmed** by this honest evaluation when rigorously articulated against candidate operations. The plausibility was forward-looking rather than verified. This is a legitimate catch under the 3b.4 plan-vs-evidence discipline — the plausibility-claim was authored by the same author-same-day that admitted the four-categories canon-object-classification; rigorous per-operation analysis was deferred to ADR-0055 by ADR-0048's own prose. ADR-0055 performs the deferred analysis honestly and reports the outcome.

Implication: Option A (10th primitive) is not warranted on earning-test grounds.

### Why Johar's "structured encounter" is discipline, not primitive

Johar's *"structured encounter"* (johar-situational-truthing), *"encounter rules"* (johar-metacognition-stack), and *"encounter engineering"* (johar-ecology-of-courage) describe **discipline-about-how-coordination-should-happen**, not a missing coordination-verb. The Johar-native reading names a *quality of event-discipline* — disciplined-conversation, trust-weight-design, perceptual-relational-alive-conditions — that Spore canon can acknowledge at framing-note or pattern-library layer without primitive-inflation. This parallels how ADR-0045 admitted `care-commoning` as cross-cutting doctrine rather than primitive, and how ADR-0048 admitted expressive/constructed power as modes-across-primitives rather than primitives — Johar-native concerns routed to the canon-object-class that fits their structural role, not elevated to primitive-status by default.

## Decision

**Admit `encounter` as derived glossary slug. Author shared framing-note articulating Encounter as composition of existing primitives within bounded Field-conditions. Park federation-encounter as pattern-library candidate for future separate-plan admission under operational-pressure triggers. Preserve 9-primitive roster and 4-category canon object-class inventory.** (Option D + E.1 hybrid from decision-brief.)

- **Authors shared framing-note** at `docs/research/connections/canon-framing-encounter-as-composition.md` articulating Encounter = Signal (invitation / reframing transmission) + Joint-commitment (attendance-pledge / shared orientation) + Intent (pre-event surfacing / in-event shaping) + Evidence (in-event attestation / post-event capture) within bounded Field-conditions (temporal-spatial scope-conditions; rule-in-use scaffolding per ADR-0046). ### What this ADR does NOT do

- **Does NOT admit Encounter as 10th primitive.** Earning-test (a) fails at verb-primitive level per Step 0.5 honest evaluation; primitive-admission would violate parsimony-as-earning-test-outcome discipline (ADR-0048).
- **Does NOT admit Encounter as cross-cutting doctrine** (Option-space analogue-to-ADR-0045 care-commoning). Category-fit is weaker: doctrines are lenses applied across primitives (structurally-constitutive practice-dispositions); structured-encounter is an event-discipline, not a practice-disposition running across all primitives. If future work establishes a "structured-encounter-commoning" disposition meeting doctrine-category criteria, a separate ADR may reconsider.
- **Does NOT admit Encounter as mode-across-primitives** (Option-space analogue-to-ADR-0048 expressive/constructed modes). Modes are qualities of primitive-operation (how they operate under stress/plurality/emergence); Encounter is an event-scope-shape rather than a quality of operation across primitives.
- **Does NOT extend Field with temporal-spatial-co-presence sub-stratification** (Option-space analogue-to-ADR-0046 rule-level-stratification). Co-presence-establish's common-knowledge-of-mutual-attention is substrate-like, but admitting a Field-sub-stratification for a single-item structure is premature without cross-tradition support beyond Johar + common-knowledge-philosophy. Parks as separate-plan candidate.
- **Does NOT admit federation-encounter as pattern-library entry now.** Pattern-library is embryonic (only `governance-memory` pattern currently resident per project-vision.md §Four-categories); creating a second pattern while pattern-library-spec itself is under-specified risks shape-without-scaffolding. Parks as triggered-admission candidate for future separate-plan work.
- **Does NOT modify ADR-0044, ADR-0046, ADR-0047, ADR-0048, ADR-0049, ADR-0050, ADR-0051, ADR-0052, ADR-0053, or ADR-0054.** All preserved structurally intact. Phase 3b.1–3b.8 closures preserved.
- **Does NOT touch individual primitive-bullets** (Signal / Joint-commitment / Intent / Evidence) in project-vision.md or constitutional-artifacts.md per operator directive (preserves recently-refactored readability).
- **Does NOT do IC/PM coordinated grammar alignment for the new `encounter` derived slug.** That remains queued as post-Phase-3b bundled alignment work.

## Maturation triggers for federation-encounter pattern-library admission

If one or more of the following triggers fires, a future separate-plan cycle may re-evaluate whether federation-encounter warrants pattern-library admission. Triggers are operationally falsifiable per the ADR-0054 discipline:

### Trigger E-1 — Pattern-library-spec declared

Pattern-library object-class is operationalized with a clear spec (what a pattern file looks like, where pattern files live, what metadata patterns carry, how patterns compose with primitives/doctrines/modes). Once pattern-library infrastructure exists, federation-encounter becomes a natural admission candidate. Evidence-class: `docs/patterns/` or equivalent directory-with-spec exists and has at least one non-governance-memory pattern admitted.

### Trigger E-2 — Multi-instance operational-pressure reports

Three or more independent instance-family reports document that federation-encounter-recurring-structure is being re-invented ad-hoc rather than composed from pattern-library admission. Evidence-class: 3+ bridge-notes / meeting-notes / evidence-captures across BKC-Octo, PM, DW, cross-federation documenting ad-hoc re-implementation of event-discipline that a federation-encounter pattern would standardize.

### Trigger E-3 — Cross-tradition convergence on event-discipline structure

Three or more independent learning-field sources (beyond Johar) converge on event-discipline / co-presence-design / federation-event-engineering as a structurally-recurring pattern in coordination literature. Cross-tradition corroboration that the concern is structural rather than Johar-idiosyncratic. Evidence-class: 3+ bridge-notes or capstone citations from distinct non-Johar traditions treating event-discipline as coordination-grammar-relevant.

### Trigger E-4 — Operational grammar-insufficiency documented

Instance-family field-report documents a coordination-event-recurrence where Spore's existing primitives + Field-conditions fail to articulate what happened and a pattern-level articulation would have prevented the coordination-failure. Evidence-class: bridge-note with specific instance-failure-documentation + demonstrated-insufficiency argument.

### Trigger E-5 — Field-sub-stratification separate-plan advances

A separate-plan investigation of Field temporal-spatial-co-presence sub-stratification (the ADR-0046-parallel move this ADR explicitly declines to make now) matures to the point where co-presence-Field-condition admission would stabilize encounter-as-composition into a Field-hosted regularity. At that point federation-encounter pattern-library admission becomes co-admission-candidate with the Field-extension.

### Trigger discipline

Any *one* trigger firing is sufficient to open a future federation-encounter-pattern-library-admission cycle. Triggers are gates on re-evaluation, not gates on admission — a re-evaluation cycle may still decline, admit at pattern-library layer, or recommend Field-sub-stratification instead.
