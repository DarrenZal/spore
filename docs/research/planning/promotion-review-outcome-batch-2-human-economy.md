## 4. Outcome Notes

**Outcome:** Approved with explicit qualification. The family moves to `ready` status.

**Binding Qualification:** The confirmed canon-safe layer is strictly limited to three mechanisms:

1. **Diagnostic** — wage and transaction grammar breaks under discovery (Hart, Williamson, Simon).
2. **Standing** — participation requires security, capability, and membership conditions (Polanyi, Standing, Sen, Ostrom).
3. **Broad replacement principle** — governance should vary by the epistemic character of work (March, stage-gate, pharmaceutical governance).

These three layers, and only these three, constitute the confirmed-mechanism layer that is safe for canon.

**What must remain outside the confirmed layer:**

- **CLC** — operational precedent and design lineage only. It cannot appear as independent corroboration or as a fourth confirmed mechanism. CLC demonstrates that commitment-based coordination is operationally viable at scale, but because Spore acknowledges CLC as a design influence, CLC evidence cannot be presented as independent convergence.
- **Four-type commitment-signature taxonomy** (delivery, discovery, stewardship, risk) — design hypothesis only. Two full corroboration passes found no external analog. It enters as Johar's engineering on confirmed components, not as externally confirmed structure.
- **Standing-plus-differentiation composition** — design hypothesis only. No production system combines a security floor with epistemic commitment-type differentiation.
- **Spore grammar parallel** — explanatory design context only. The structural overlap between Spore's commitment grammar and Johar's commitment-economy account is real but cannot serve as confirming evidence. Spore was built with awareness of this conceptual territory.
- **Operational-viability language** — the reviewer specifically flagged that Section 4A and the draft canon entry must be watched so that operational-viability language does not broaden the confirmed layer beyond diagnostic + standing + broad replacement principle.

**What this means now:** The family is at `ready` status. A canon entry can be drafted using the review packet's draft language, but the draft must be refined to ensure operational-viability phrasing (particularly around CLC's production evidence) does not blur the boundary between "commitment-based coordination works at scale" (operational precedent, safe to note) and "this confirms the commitment-economy theory" (overclaim, not safe).

**What still remains outside approval:** The four-type taxonomy as confirmed structure. The standing-plus-differentiation composition as demonstrated architecture. CLC as independent corroboration. The Spore parallel as evidence. Discovery-specific intensification beyond what Hart/Williamson/Simon establish. The wage-as-problem framing reversal beyond the confirmed diagnostic.
