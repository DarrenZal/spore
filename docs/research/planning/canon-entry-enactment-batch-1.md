## 9. Guardrails

- Promotion into canon does not validate every surrounding synthesis claim. The synthesis notes contain convergence analysis, claim registers, open questions, and Johar-distinctive integration that remain research artifacts. The canon entries carry only the confirmed-mechanism core plus explicitly tagged contributions.
- Explicit qualification must remain visible in future reuse. Any document that references the promoted entries should preserve the distinction between confirmed mechanism and design response. The phrases "structural approaches," "designed to resist," and "design responses to the confirmed mechanism" are load-bearing — they should not be silently upgraded to stronger language.
- Promoted entries should not silently absorb Johar's full integrated vocabulary. "Orchestration over dominance," "imaginary identity," "context metabolism," the 4-dimensional taxonomy, and "the real infrastructure" remain outside canon. Future work may promote these with their own evidence, but they should not drift into canon language through indirect reference.
- Adjacent ready/defer families remain unchanged. The 18 synthesis notes at `promotion_status: defer` were not touched. No family's status should change without its own review pass.
- Future edits to the promoted entries should preserve the same evidentiary discipline. Adding evidence, refining wording, or expanding scope should go through the same decomposition (confirmed mechanism / design contribution / design hypothesis) and review process that produced this batch.
