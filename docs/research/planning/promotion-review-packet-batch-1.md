**Each family in this packet contains three layers:**

**What you are being asked to judge:**

For each of the 3 families, decide whether the draft canon entry language is defensible enough to move from `promotion_status: defer` to a status that allows the claim to inform grammar design — while preserving explicit tagging of what is confirmed vs what is contributed vs what is hypothesis.

**What should count as promotion-worthy:**

A family is promotion-worthy when:
1. The structural mechanism is confirmed by independent external sources (all 3 meet this).
2. The Johar-specific remainder is clearly identified and tagged (all 3 have been decomposed in the prep work).
3. The draft entry language is narrow enough that nothing unconfirmed is presented as confirmed.
4. Family boundaries are stable and the promotion does not absorb adjacent families' territory.

**What should remain explicitly qualified:**

- Johar's specific design vocabulary (taxonomies, named modes, named concepts) should be introduced as "Johar's design contribution" or "the grammar's design response," never as "externally confirmed structure."
- Specific parameters (e.g. 6-9 month half-life) should be flagged as testable hypotheses.
- Claims about Spore's own mechanisms working (claim lifecycle resists closure, commitment protocols achieve consequence-binding) should be tagged as design intent, not as demonstrated fact.

**Why this packet is intentionally narrow:**

Three families, not five. The two excluded families (holding-complexity-beyond-control, human-economy-and-commitment) have wider Johar-specific remainders or unresolved replacement-mechanism gaps. This narrow batch establishes the tagging precedent with the cleanest cases. The harder families follow in a second batch once the format is proven.

## 4. Family Dossiers

### 4.1 Legitimacy-and-Shared-Consequence

#### A. What Is Confirmed

- Institutions that structurally couple authority to consequence-bearing are substantially more viable than those where authority and consequence separate. (Ostrom: design principles 2/3/4/5; Baggio 2016: 69 cases, successful commons averaged 8.7/10 principles vs failed commons 4.3/10; DeCaro 2024: experimental confirmation that severing enforcement from participation produces legitimacy collapse.)
- No single governance axis suffices; multiple overlapping legitimacy sources are structurally necessary. (Ostrom: polycentric governance; Beetham: three irreducible dimensions; Scharpf: input and output legitimacy both required.)
- When authority separates from consequence, self-reinforcing escalation produces institutional failure. (Hirschman: exit-voice death spiral — quality-conscious members exit, removing the principal agents of accountability; Scott: simplification-failure-further-simplification pattern across 4 historical cases.)
- Nepal irrigation systems provide controlled case evidence: farmer-managed systems (authority and consequence coupled) achieved 70%+ high performance vs government-managed systems (authority and consequence separated) at 40%, despite the government systems having superior physical infrastructure.

#### B. What Is Johar's Design Contribution

- The 4-dimensional taxonomy (democracy, stewardship, experimentation, radical markets) as a specific architecture for instantiating polycentric legitimacy. Externally confirmed: legitimacy must be multi-dimensional. Johar's contribution: these particular 4 dimensions, named and composed.
- "Orchestration over dominance" as a named coordination mode for holding plural legitimacy together. Implied by Ostrom's mutual adjustment among overlapping centres; Johar names it and proposes it as a design orientation.
- The control-surveillance-energy spiral as a named escalation pattern. Scott and Hirschman independently describe the escalation mechanism; Johar names the specific spiral and extends it to include the energetic dimension.

#### C. What Is Still Hypothesis or Distinctive Integration

- Civic economy as a third pole of organising — autonomous from market and state, capable of interrupting the control spiral. Ostrom's commons are adjacent but not framed this way. This is a design hypothesis.
- The precise boundary between consequence-bearing and coercive over-binding within commitment grammar — acknowledged as unresolved in the synthesis note itself.

#### D. Draft Canon Entry Language

> Institutions become viable when authority and consequence are structurally coupled — when those who shape decisions are bound into the outcomes those decisions produce. This coupling must be polycentric: no single legitimacy source (democratic mandate, technocratic expertise, market mechanism) suffices on its own; multiple overlapping legitimacy dimensions are structurally necessary. When authority and consequence separate, institutions enter self-reinforcing degradation: consequence-bearing members who could provide corrective feedback lose voice or exit, insulating authority-holders from the outcomes they produce. [mechanism confirmed: Ostrom, Scharpf, Hirschman, Scott; experimental: DeCaro 2024; configural: Baggio 2016; case: Nepal irrigation] Johar contributes a specific 4-dimensional taxonomy (democracy, stewardship, experimentation, radical markets) and the coordination mode of "orchestration over dominance" as design vocabulary for instantiating polycentric legitimacy within coordination infrastructure. [design contribution] The claim that a civic economy third pole can interrupt control spirals is a design hypothesis awaiting production evidence. [design hypothesis]

#### E. Reviewer Questions

- Does the draft entry language clearly distinguish the confirmed mechanism (authority-consequence coupling, polycentric requirement, escalation-when-broken) from the Johar taxonomy (4 specific dimensions, "orchestration")? If a reader encounters this entry, will they know which parts are externally grounded?
- Should the 4-dimensional taxonomy be introduced in the canon entry at all, or should the entry name only "polycentric legitimacy" and leave the specific dimensions for a grammar-design document?
- Is Scott's evidence doing double duty? Scott appeared in the legitimacy corroboration (authority-consequence separation) and in the holding-complexity corroboration (authoritarian compression). The two claims are distinct, but the reviewer should confirm that the legitimacy entry does not depend on the complexity claim.

#### F. Suggested Disposition

**Promote with explicit qualification.** This is the strongest family in the batch. The mechanism is the most thoroughly corroborated of all 5 priority families (4 traditions, experimental evidence, configural analysis, controlled case comparison). The Johar-specific remainder is the narrowest: vocabulary and taxonomy for a confirmed mechanism, not structural claims that go beyond the evidence. The entry language is defensible as written. The main qualification: the 4-dimensional taxonomy should be introduced as Johar's design vocabulary, not as an externally confirmed architecture. A reviewer who accepts this framing can approve promotion.

### 4.2 Linguistic-Closure

#### A. What Is Confirmed

- Categories, paradigms, stabilised facts, and enacted sensemaking produce a sense of sufficiency that terminates inquiry — not because truth has been found but because the category feels adequate. (Peirce: 3 of 4 belief-fixation methods are self-sealing; Kuhn: paradigms determine what can be perceived as a problem before truth-testing; Latour: black boxing stabilises facts into apparent objectivity; Weick: organisations treat enacted categories as given reality.)
- This is a structural property of language and categories operating in institutional contexts, not a cognitive error attributable to individual bias. (All 4 traditions: the mechanism operates at the institutional level; it determines what can be noticed before individual cognition engages.)
- The corrective must be institutional — structural revisability, recursive inquiry, error-finding incentives — not individual cognitive improvement. (Peirce: only the scientific method has built-in self-correction; Weick: HRO principles as institutional anti-closure; Nosek et al.: pre-registration and registered reports as structural reform in science.)
- The replication crisis demonstrates both the failure mode (publication categories became self-sealing, producing a reproducibility crisis affecting 60%+ of studies in psychology) and the structural reform (pre-registration, registered reports, open data, adversarial collaborations).

#### B. What Is Johar's Design Contribution

- *Naming* as the upstream mechanism — the claim that the act of giving something a word is the primary closure trigger. External sources confirm that categories terminate inquiry; Johar adds the precision that naming is the upstream act that produces the category. Well-supported but stated at a level of specificity the external sources do not independently reach.
- The claim lifecycle, discourse graph, and attestation decay as specific anti-closure mechanisms for coordination infrastructure. The replication crisis reform provides a domain-specific precedent (structural mechanisms that keep inquiry open in science); Johar generalises the principle to all institutional domains through specific grammar mechanisms.
- The three-concept sequence (category inadequacy → linguistic closure → recursive intelligence) as a unified failure-mode-and-corrective framework. Johar's design architecture for connecting the confirmed condition (closure) to its corrective (recursion) through a specific grammar infrastructure.

#### C. What Is Still Hypothesis or Distinctive Integration

- LLM amplification of closure risk (cheap coherence, high oracle density, automated language production). Plausible risk analysis but all corroborating sources predate the era. No empirical evidence specific to this claim.
- Whether Spore's specific mechanisms (claim lifecycle, discourse graph, attestation decay) actually resist closure at general institutional scale. The corrective *principle* (institutional, not individual) is confirmed; the specific mechanisms are untested.
- Whether structural mechanisms ensure *perceptibility* of closure (noticing that a category has become self-sealing), not just *revisability* of claims (being able to change them once noticed). The synthesis note identifies this as an open limitation.

#### D. Draft Canon Entry Language

> Language compresses reality into categories that produce a structural sense of sufficiency, terminating inquiry before truth evaluation begins. This is not a cognitive error but a property of how categories operate in institutional contexts: paradigms determine what can be perceived as a problem, stabilised facts become invisible through their own success, and organisations treat enacted categories as given reality. Four independent traditions confirm this mechanism. The corrective is institutional, not individual: structural revisability, error-finding incentives, and recursive inquiry keep categories open to revision. The replication crisis demonstrates both the failure mode and the structural reform. [mechanism confirmed: Peirce, Kuhn, Latour, Weick; case: replication crisis] Johar contributes the specific claim that naming is the upstream trigger, and proposes the claim lifecycle, discourse graph, and attestation decay as general-purpose anti-closure infrastructure for coordination grammar. [design contribution] Whether these specific mechanisms resist closure at institutional scale, and whether automated language production amplifies closure risk, are open design hypotheses. [design hypothesis]

#### E. Reviewer Questions

- Is the corrective principle ("must be institutional") being overclaimed as confirmation of Spore's specific mechanisms? The entry must clearly distinguish: "the corrective must be institutional" (confirmed by Peirce, Weick, Nosek) from "Spore's claim lifecycle is an effective anti-closure mechanism" (design hypothesis).
- Should the LLM amplification claim appear in the canon entry at all, or should it be deferred entirely until empirical evidence exists? Including it as a flagged hypothesis is honest but creates an entry that partly rests on a claim about a condition that post-dates all the corroborating sources.
- Linguistic-closure crosses projects (Spore + IC, 8 governance clusters). Does promotion in Spore canon automatically apply to IC, or should IC handle its own promotion review?
- Is the "naming as upstream mechanism" claim a precision-addition to the confirmed mechanism (acceptable as design contribution) or a substantive additional structural claim (needing its own corroboration)?

#### F. Suggested Disposition

**Promote with explicit qualification.** The mechanism is the cleanest match across all 5 corroboration runs — 4 traditions independently naming the same stopping-rule structure. The corrective principle is confirmed. The qualification: Spore's specific anti-closure mechanisms (claim lifecycle, discourse graph, attestation decay) should be introduced as the grammar's design response to the confirmed mechanism, not as confirmed infrastructure. The LLM amplification claim should be flagged as hypothesis. A reviewer who accepts this framing — confirmed mechanism + tagged design response + flagged hypothesis — can approve promotion. The entry is more cautious than the full synthesis note, which is intentional.

### 4.3 Relational-Topology-and-Context-Design

#### A. What Is Confirmed

- Organisational contexts, material arrangements, and network topologies actively generate relation, identity, perception, and power — they are not neutral containers. (Suchman: situation generates action; Orlikowski: social and material constitutively entangled; Dale & Burrell: "the organisation of space is simultaneously the organisation of power.")
- Network position generates outcomes controlling for individual attributes: the same individual in different network positions produces different results. (Burt 2004: blind evaluation; structural holes generate information access and idea quality independent of the person.)
- Context variables predict coordination quality more strongly than composition variables. (Edmondson 1999 / Google Project Aristotle 2015: 180+ teams, 250+ attributes; psychological safety is a group-level emergent property generated by norms and interaction patterns, not by team composition.)
- Structural decay of relational grounding is the default without active maintenance. Topology changes produce measurable coordination effects holding people constant. (Roberts & Dunbar 2015: longitudinal decay; Allen Curve: communication drops exponentially with distance; Yang/Microsoft 2021: remote shift → 25% drop in cross-group collaboration among 61k employees; Polzer et al. 2006: 3-3 geographic split generates competing faultlines.)

#### B. What Is Johar's Design Contribution

- "Imaginary identity" as a named concept for the emergent sense of "we" produced by relational density patterns. The phenomenon is demonstrated (Polzer: spatial arrangement generates competing subgroup identities; Hatch & Schultz: organisational identity emerges from relational processes). The naming and the claim that it is "always partial, positional, and structurally mediated" are Johar's.
- Context design as a named institutional design discipline — paradigmatically distinct from process design or formal structure design. The evidence supports the distinction (changing context changes outcomes independent of process changes), but the naming of "context design" as a standalone discipline is Johar's integration.
- The integrative thesis that relational topology is "the real infrastructure of institutions" — combining situated action, structural holes, sociomateriality, and psychological safety into a single design framework. Each component is independently confirmed; the unified frame is Johar's.

#### C. What Is Still Hypothesis or Distinctive Integration

- The 6-9 month alignment half-life as a specific quantitative parameter. Structural decay is well-supported but no external source provides a half-life estimate. This is a testable hypothesis awaiting longitudinal measurement.
- "Context metabolism" — the claim that contexts have incentive fields, recognition loops, and learning architectures that collectively determine what inhabitants can perceive. No external equivalent exists.
- Scaling from team-level evidence (Aristotle, Polzer, distributed-team research) to the institutional-level claim ("the real infrastructure of institutions"). Plausible but not directly demonstrated at institutional scale.

#### D. Draft Canon Entry Language

> Organisational contexts, material arrangements, and network topologies actively generate the patterns of relation, identity, perception, and power that constitute institutional life. They are not neutral containers. Context variables — norms, interaction patterns, psychological safety — predict coordination quality more strongly than composition variables. Network position generates power and information access independent of individual attributes. Structural decay of relational grounding is the default condition without active maintenance: communication drops with distance, networks silo under topology change, and faultlines emerge from arrangement, not personality. [mechanism confirmed: Suchman, Burt, Orlikowski, Edmondson/Aristotle; empirical: Project Aristotle (180+ teams), Polzer (experimental), Yang/Microsoft (61k employees), Allen Curve, Roberts & Dunbar] Johar integrates these independently confirmed components into a unified design framework: relational topology as the generative infrastructure of institutions, with "imaginary identity" (the emergent sense of "we" produced by density patterns) and context design as a named institutional discipline. [Johar-distinctive integration] The 6-9 month alignment half-life and "context metabolism" are testable design hypotheses awaiting production evidence. [design hypothesis]

#### E. Reviewer Questions

- Is the integrative thesis ("topology is the real infrastructure") presented with enough qualification, or does the draft entry language risk reading as if this integration is an externally confirmed finding? The components are strong; the integration is Johar's.
- Should the 6-9 month half-life appear in the canon entry as a flagged hypothesis, or should it be deferred to a grammar-design document? Including it invites readers to treat it as a design parameter before it has been measured.
- Does the entry absorb any evidence that belongs to the agility-contribution family? Alignment-decay sits at the topology-agility boundary. The decay mechanism is topological (context change → relational erosion); the consequence (loss of coordination capacity) is agility's territory. The entry must not claim the consequence.
- Is "imaginary identity" sufficiently supported to enter as a design concept, or is it too far from the confirmed evidence (Polzer demonstrates topology-generated subgroup identity but does not name it "imaginary identity")?

#### F. Suggested Disposition

**Promote with explicit qualification.** The components are the most empirically grounded of any family in the batch — controlled experiments, blind evaluations, natural experiments with tens of thousands of subjects, and longitudinal studies. The qualification: the integrative thesis ("the real infrastructure"), "imaginary identity," and "context metabolism" must be clearly tagged as Johar-distinctive integration and design contribution, not as externally confirmed structure. The 6-9 month half-life must be flagged as hypothesis. This is the most cautious promotion in the batch because the integration claim is the largest, but the component evidence is strong enough to support introducing the integration as tagged Johar contribution. A reviewer who is comfortable with the distinction between confirmed components and Johar integration can approve promotion.

## 5. Cross-Family Boundary Notes

### Linguistic-closure vs civic-truth

**What must remain distinct:** Linguistic closure is the *pathology* — how categories become self-sealing and terminate inquiry. Civic truth is the *commons condition* — designed institutional capacity for holding plural, revisable truth claims. Closure names the failure mode; civic-truth names the institutional prevention.

**What evidence transfers:** Latour's "matters of concern" (reopening settled facts) sits near the boundary — it is both an anti-closure mechanism and a civic-truth design principle. If civic-truth is later corroborated and promoted, Latour could serve both families for different sub-claims, but the attribution must be explicit.

**What should not be collapsed:** Promoting linguistic-closure should not create implicit pressure to promote civic-truth. Civic-truth has not been corroborated and should not ride on linguistic-closure's momentum. The canon entry for closure should reference the boundary ("civic-truth names the institutional condition that resists this failure mode") without absorbing civic-truth's territory.

### Linguistic-closure vs perceptual-fidelity

**What must remain distinct:** Closure is about *categories foreclosing what can be noticed* — a language/framing mechanism. Fidelity is about *the depth and quality of sensing contact* with reality before and through abstraction. Closure narrows perception by making categories feel sufficient; fidelity concerns whether sensing preserves relational richness through the abstraction process.

**What evidence transfers:** Kuhn's incommensurability touches both — scientists in different paradigms literally perceive different phenomena. The mechanism (paradigmatic categories shape perception) is closure; the consequence (degraded sensing quality) touches fidelity. This boundary touch is narrow.

**What should not be collapsed:** A canon entry for closure should not claim to address sensing quality. The two families address different structural questions even though closure can degrade fidelity.

### Legitimacy-and-shared-consequence vs relational-topology-and-context-design

**What must remain distinct:** Legitimacy asks *who has authority and who bears consequences* — a political question about standing and outcome-binding. Topology asks *how the pattern of connection generates what relations, identities, and perceptions are possible* — an infrastructure question about generative arrangement. An institution can have good topology with poor legitimacy (well-connected but authority and consequence split) or poor topology with good legitimacy (legitimate but relationally fractured).

**What evidence transfers:** The spatial-politics source in the legitimacy synthesis note links the two: "differential relational density produces political asymmetry." Ostrom's Principle 7 (rights to organise) also bridges them. If both families are promoted, these boundary touches should be acknowledged but the claims should be attributed to their primary family.

**What should not be collapsed:** A legitimacy entry should not absorb topology evidence about how arrangements generate patterns. A topology entry should not absorb legitimacy evidence about authority-consequence binding. The mechanisms are genuinely different even when they affect the same institutions.

### Relational-topology-and-context-design vs agility-contribution-and-organizing

**What must remain distinct:** Topology names *what arrangements generate* — the relational patterns, identities, and perceptions that emerge from infrastructure. Agility names *how to maintain adaptive coordination capacity* over time. The alignment-decay claim (trust erodes without maintenance) sits at their boundary: the *mechanism* of decay is topological (context change → relational erosion); the *consequence* (loss of coordination capacity) is agility's territory.

**What evidence transfers:** Roberts & Dunbar's decay evidence, the distributed-team literature, and the Allen Curve could serve both families. The topology entry should claim the mechanism (what causes decay); agility should claim the response (how to maintain coordination capacity despite decay).

**What should not be collapsed:** The topology entry should not claim to address how to maintain coordination capacity — that is agility's territory. The agility family has not been corroborated and should not absorb topology's decay evidence as its own.

## 8. Guardrails

1. **Confirmed mechanism is the upper bound of what can be asserted cleanly right now.** The draft canon entry language is intentionally narrower than the synthesis notes. The synthesis notes contain the full Johar analysis; the canon entries carry only what the corroboration evidence supports plus explicitly tagged contributions and hypotheses. Narrowness is a feature, not a limitation.

2. **Johar-distinctive integration may be valuable without yet being canon-ready in its own right.** "Imaginary identity," "context metabolism," and the "real infrastructure" thesis are plausible, coherent, and non-trivially derived. They are also Johar's. They enter canon as tagged contributions — proudly, not apologetically — but with tags that prevent future readers from treating them as externally confirmed.

3. **"Promote with explicit qualification" is not a weak outcome.** All 3 families receive this disposition. It means: the mechanism enters canon as confirmed; the design vocabulary enters as contribution; the hypotheses enter as flagged open questions. This is more honest and more useful than either "promote everything" (overclaiming) or "promote nothing until production evidence exists" (stalling on strong evidence).

4. **A stronger family should not pull a weaker one over the line.** Legitimacy's strength does not validate linguistic-closure's corrective claim. Relational-topology's empirical base does not validate legitimacy's 4-dimensional taxonomy. Each family is reviewed on its own evidence. The batch structure is for efficiency, not for mutual reinforcement.

5. **The review should reward defensibility, not elegance.** The synthesis notes are well-crafted. The corroboration evidence is strong. But the canon should carry only what is defensible, with everything else explicitly tagged. A plain entry that a reader can trust is more valuable than a beautiful entry that obscures the evidence boundaries.
