## 4. Refined Section 4A

**Exact replacement text for Section "4A. What Is Confirmed":**

### A. What Is Confirmed

- **Control failure under manifest complexity is a provable structural condition.** A regulator cannot govern a system whose variety exceeds the regulator's own representational capacity (Ashby, Law of Requisite Variety). Beer operationalises this for institutions: institutions default to variety reduction when overwhelmed because they structurally cannot do otherwise. Two independent traditions (information theory, institutional cybernetics) converge on the same structural proof.

- **Authoritarian compression is an empirically documented default response.** When institutions face complexity exceeding their regulatory capacity, one observable default is to narrow degrees of freedom -- enforcing legibility, simplifying coordination, constraining variation, destroying local adaptive knowledge. This produces short-term stability while reducing adaptive capacity. Scott, Tainter, and Beer document this from three independent traditions with detailed historical cases (Soviet collectivisation, Tanzanian ujamaa, scientific forestry, Roman taxation).

- **Chaotic decomposition is the other empirically documented default response.** When the costs of institutional complexity exceed its benefits, the other observable default is loss of higher-order coordinating structures -- reorganisation at a lower level of complexity. Tainter defines collapse as "suddenly smaller, simpler, less stratified." Holling's release phase and poverty trap provide ecological evidence. Two independent traditions (historical sociology, systems ecology) with multiple civilisational cases.

- **Adaptive coordination is the independently confirmed general response requirement.** Navigating evolving conditions through iterative sensing and response rather than enforcing predefined outcomes. Snowden's probe-sense-respond in the complex domain directly matches. Folke's adaptive governance confirms the finding at the institutional level. Two independent traditions (complexity practice, institutional ecology). This claim enters the confirmed layer as the general finding only, not as a claim about the specific capacities, institutional forms, or evaluative criteria of the response.

**What changed and why:**

1. **Removed the routing clause from bullet 4.** The previous text introduced "deliberate institutional construction" by name inside the confirmed section, even though it was routing the concept to Section 4B. The routing is correct but should be performed by the structure of the document (4B exists for design contributions), not by naming the excluded concept inside the confirmed block.
2. **Replaced the explicit five-capacity guardrail with a general scope limiter.** The previous text named "five named capacities" and "five-capacity architecture" inside the confirmed section as negative references. The revised text limits scope without naming the excluded architecture: "not as a claim about the specific capacities, institutional forms, or evaluative criteria of the response."
3. **No substantive claims were added, removed, or changed.** All four confirmed mechanisms remain exactly as the reviewer approved. The refinement tightens phrasing to prevent paraphrase drift; it does not alter the diagnostic.

## 5. Refined Draft Canon Entry Language

**Exact replacement text for Section "4D. Draft Canon Entry Language":**

> Control-based coordination fails under manifest complexity as a provable structural condition: a regulator cannot govern a system whose variety exceeds the regulator's own representational capacity (Ashby). **What changed and why:**

## 6. Keep / Cut Guidance

### Keep

- "provable structural condition" -- accurate for Ashby's mathematical theorem
- "a regulator cannot govern a system whose variety exceeds the regulator's own representational capacity" -- direct statement of the Law of Requisite Variety
- "enforcing legibility, simplifying coordination, constraining variation" -- Scott's exact characterisation of authoritarian high modernism
- "suddenly smaller, simpler, less stratified" -- Tainter's definition of collapse
- "not disorder, but insufficient coordination relative to existing interdependence" -- accurate restatement that avoids the basin-competition framing
- "navigating evolving conditions through iterative sensing and response rather than enforcing predefined outcomes" -- diagnostic-safe definition of adaptive coordination, independent of five-capacity architecture
- "independently confirmed general response requirement" -- correctly scoped: the finding is general, not specific to any capacity architecture
- "corroborated (Folke, March) but belongs to the design-contribution layer" -- correctly tags the deliberate-construction claim
- "the corroboration establishes the institutional requirement without specifying the architecture" -- the key sentence that maintains the boundary

### Cut Or Avoid

- **"adaptive coordination" as one of five capacities** -- In canon-facing text, adaptive coordination may only appear as the general response requirement. It must never be listed as one element of a five-element architecture or as "the first of five capacities."
- **"deliberate institutional construction" inside the confirmed layer** -- This phrase belongs only in the design-contribution section. It must not appear in 4A or in any text tagged `[mechanism confirmed]`.
- **"energetic/material constraint" in the canon entry** -- Must not appear in any form: not as a core pillar, not as secondary context, not as a thermodynamic qualifier. The reviewer's "stay out of the approved core entirely" is binding. If energetic constraint must be discussed, it belongs in the synthesis note or the review dossier, never in the canon entry.
- **"five-capacity architecture" or enumeration of the five capacities** -- Must not appear in the canon entry. The design architecture should be referenced generically ("the specific design architecture") without naming its components.
- **"fork structure" or "fork in the system" or "third path"** -- The fork integration is Johar-distinctive. The canon entry names two default responses and one confirmed alternative direction. It does not name a "fork" or a "third path."
- **"civilisational optionality"** -- Must not appear in the canon entry. Taleb + Sen assembled at civilisational scale is Johar's synthesis. The diagnostic does not need an evaluative criterion.
- **"reflexive-constraint clause" or "must be built under conditions that actively constrain its construction"** -- Must not appear in the canon entry. Folke demonstrates the difficulty; the named clause is Johar's.
- **"basin-competition" or "competing attractor basins"** -- The diagnostic can state two default responses exist. It cannot assert they are competing basins. Tainter sees them as sequential; the basin-competition framing is Johar's.
- **"meta-goal stewardship" or "meta-goal/object-goal distinction"** -- Must not appear. March provides grown goals; the structural category is Johar's.
- **Dutch Water Boards inside `[mechanism confirmed]` tag** -- The case straddles confirmed and design-contribution layers. It should appear under the design-contribution sentence where deliberate institutional construction is discussed, not under the confirmed-mechanism tag.

## 10. Guardrails

- **Reviewer qualification is binding.** The four-finding boundary and the explicit exclusion list from the batch-3 review outcome are the governing constraints for all downstream work. Wording refinement does not create new authority; it tightens language within existing authority.
- **Wording discipline matters as much as evidence discipline.** A correctly tagged claim that reads as part of the confirmed layer in natural paraphrase has failed. The test is not "is the tag correct?" but "will a reader who drops the tags still understand the boundary?"
- **Deliberate institutional construction must not be recoded as confirmed mechanism.** The corroboration (Folke, March) is real. The classification is design contribution. The temptation is to say "the requirement for deliberate construction is confirmed" -- which is true at the corroboration level but crosses the reviewer's boundary at the classification level. The binding constraint: "design contribution only, not confirmed mechanism."
- **Energetic/material constraint must not enter the approved core.** The thermodynamic foundation (Tainter, Georgescu-Roegen, Odum) is solid. The reviewer says it stays out entirely. If it must appear anywhere, it is in the synthesis note as background context, never in the canon entry.
- **Later enactment should be narrower than the synthesis note, not broader.** The synthesis note contains the full holding-complexity architecture (five capacities, fork framing, civilisational optionality, energetic constraint, meta-goal stewardship). The canon entry must draw only from the diagnostic layer. The refined 4D wording in this memo is the ceiling for what the enactment can assert, not a floor.
- **Adaptive coordination must remain the general finding.** In the confirmed layer, adaptive coordination is what Snowden independently confirms: probe-sense-respond in the complex domain. It is not one of five capacities, not a component of an architecture, not a gateway to the five-capacity package. If the canon entry is later paraphrased as "Snowden confirms the first of five capacities Johar names," the enactment has failed.
