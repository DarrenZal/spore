## 4. Family Dossier

### A. What Is Confirmed

- **Control failure under manifest complexity is a provable structural condition.** A regulator cannot govern a system whose variety exceeds the regulator's own representational capacity. This is a mathematical theorem (Ashby, Law of Requisite Variety), not an observation or political claim. Beer operationalises it for institutions: institutions default to variety reduction when overwhelmed because they structurally cannot do otherwise. Two independent traditions (information theory, institutional cybernetics) converge on the same structural proof.

- **Authoritarian compression is an empirically documented default response.** When institutions face complexity exceeding their regulatory capacity, one observable default is to narrow degrees of freedom — enforce legibility, simplify coordination, constrain variation, destroy local adaptive knowledge. This produces short-term stability while reducing adaptive capacity. Scott names it precisely as "authoritarian high modernism." Beer names "default strategy of variety reduction." Tainter documents Diocletian's intensification. Three independent traditions (political theory/STS, cybernetics, historical sociology) with detailed historical cases (Soviet collectivisation, Tanzanian ujamaa, scientific forestry, Roman taxation).

- **Chaotic decomposition is the other empirically documented default response.** When the costs of institutional complexity exceed its benefits, the other observable default is loss of higher-order coordinating structures — reorganisation at a lower level of complexity. Tainter defines collapse as "suddenly smaller, simpler, less stratified" — explicitly not random chaos but insufficient coordination relative to existing interdependence. Holling's release phase and poverty trap provide ecological evidence. Two independent traditions (historical sociology, systems ecology) with multiple civilisational cases (Roman Empire, Maya, Chaco Canyon).

- **Adaptive coordination is the independently confirmed general response requirement.** Navigating evolving conditions through iterative sensing and response rather than enforcing predefined outcomes. Snowden's probe-sense-respond in the complex domain directly matches: "Leaders who try to impose order in a complex context will fail." Folke's adaptive governance supports the general finding at the institutional level; the further claim that this requires deliberate institutional construction belongs in Section 4B (design contribution). Two independent traditions (complexity practice, institutional ecology). **This claim belongs in the confirmed layer only as the general finding** — not as one of five named capacities, not as a component of the five-capacity architecture, and not as a claim about the institutional form of the response.

### B. What Is Johar's Design Contribution

- **Deliberate institutional construction of a "third path."** The institutional-level claim that a response beyond both default attractors requires deliberate design — institutional entrepreneurs, shadow networks, bridging organisations, multi-year preparation — is corroborated by Folke (Kristianstad Vattenrike case) and supported by March (exploration capacity must be institutionally protected against exploitation drift). The narrowly confirmable version: "a response beyond both defaults is structurally possible and requires deliberate institutional construction." The naming of this as a structured "third path" with a specific architecture is where the claim crosses from confirmed mechanism into design contribution. The diagnostic can state the requirement; it cannot specify what the third path contains beyond adaptive coordination.

- **Energetic/material constraint as secondary context (if included at all).** The thermodynamic foundation is directly corroborated: coordination has irreducible energetic costs (Tainter), the economic process is entropy transformation (Georgescu-Roegen), energy hierarchy constrains institutional forms (Odum). Three independent traditions. The application — that a coordination grammar must operate under tighter resource constraints than distributed control — is Johar's design inference. If this appears in the diagnostic at all, it can only appear as: "coordination has thermodynamic costs and surplus is tightening, which constrains the institutional forms available for adaptive response." It cannot appear as a core pillar or a design directive.

### C. What Is Still Hypothesis Or Distinctive Integration

**Design hypothesis:**
- **Meta-goal stewardship as a structural category.** The grown-goals half is confirmed by March: goals are discovered through action, not rationally pre-specified. The meta-goal/object-goal distinction as a named structural category — conditions for goal-formation as structurally separate from object-level goals, requiring distinct institutional stewardship — has no independent match. March operates in the same space but does not name meta-goals as a structural category.
- **Basin-competition framing.** Tainter sees compression-then-decomposition as a single trajectory. Scott sees only compression. Holling uses ecological categories. The specific framing of two competing attractor basins is Johar's interpretive integration. The diagnostic can state that two default responses are empirically observable. It cannot state that they are competing basins in a structural fork.

**Johar-distinctive integration:**
- **Five-capacity architecture** (adaptive coordination, complexity-handling capacity, scaffolded relationality, interdependent sovereignty, anti-fragility through learning). No external source independently names this package. Individual capacities have external parallels; the integration is entirely Johar's.
- **Civilisational optionality as evaluative criterion.** Taleb provides optionality as a heuristic; Sen provides capability as an evaluative framework; neither operates at civilisational-institutional scale. The synthesis is Johar's.
- **Reflexive-constraint clause** ("must be built under conditions that actively constrain its construction"). Demonstrated by Folke's cases but not independently named as a structural feature.
- **Fork structure as integrated whole.** The specific three-way integration (control fails → two named attractor basins → third path requiring deliberate construction) is Johar's. The components are independently confirmed; the integration is not.

## 5. Boundary Notes

### Distinct from `legitimacy-and-shared-consequence` (promoted)

Legitimacy names what makes institutions viable: authority-consequence coupling, polycentric legitimacy, self-reinforcing escalation when coupling breaks. Holding-complexity's diagnostic names why control-based coordination fails structurally: variety overload, not authority-consequence separation. Both use Scott, but for different structural loads — variety compression (holding-complexity) versus authority-consequence separation (legitimacy). Legitimacy's experimental evidence (DeCaro), configural evidence (Baggio), and polycentric framework (Ostrom, Scharpf, Hirschman) must not enter or strengthen the diagnostic. The phenomena overlap; the structural claims are different.

### Distinct from `relational-topology-and-context-design` (promoted)

Minimal overlap at the diagnostic level. The five-capacity architecture includes "scaffolded relationality" — which is topology territory — but scaffolded relationality is in the non-reviewable remainder, not the diagnostic core. The diagnostic (control fails, two default responses, adaptive coordination required) does not address relational topology. Project Aristotle, Burt, Polzer, and Yang/Microsoft evidence stays with topology. "Context matters more than composition" (topology's finding) is not evidence for "control fails under complexity" (holding-complexity's diagnostic).

### Distinct from `human-economy-and-commitment` (promoted)

Both diagnose coordination failure under uncertainty, but at different structural layers. Human-economy addresses economic grammar: wage/transaction coordination fails because specifiability assumptions break under discovery. Holding-complexity addresses civilisational coordination logic: control-based coordination fails because complexity exceeds representational capacity. Hart, Williamson, and Simon's contract-failure evidence stays with human-economy. Ashby, Tainter, and Scott's control-failure evidence stays with holding-complexity. They converge in implication (conventional coordination forms are inadequate) but differ in mechanism and evidence base.

### Distinct from `governance-under-volatility` (deferred)

This is the highest overlap risk. Both address what happens when control-based governance breaks. The attribution rule: general-response-requirement evidence (Snowden's probe-sense-respond as a structural finding about the complex domain) goes to holding-complexity. Specific-governance-practice evidence (decision ecologies, probabilistic governance, coherence as governance success measure) stays with governance-under-volatility. If the diagnostic absorbs the governance-practice level, it collapses the family boundary and leaves governance-under-volatility with nothing to promote on its own terms.

## 9. Guardrails

**A strong diagnostic does not validate the architecture built on top of it.** Ashby proves control fails. Tainter documents the attractors. Snowden confirms adaptive coordination. None of this validates the five-capacity package, the fork/basin framing as integrated architecture, civilisational optionality as evaluative criterion, meta-goal stewardship as structural category, or the reflexive-constraint clause. Promoting the diagnostic creates a foundation; it does not retroactively confirm the design architecture.

**The five-capacity package must remain out unless directly supported.** Individual capacities have external parallels. The unified five-element architecture does not. No further literature search is expected to close this gap. The architecture is Johar's design contribution and should be evaluated on its own terms — internal coherence and production viability — not by attempting to assemble external validation for a package no external source names.

**Adaptive coordination must not silently expand into full institutional design.** The diagnostic-safe version is: "adaptive coordination is the confirmed general response requirement." In canon use, this must not become: "adaptive coordination is the first of five capacities required to hold complexity." The distinction is between a confirmed finding (Snowden independently arrives at probe-sense-respond) and a design architecture (adaptive coordination as one element of a five-element package). If the former drifts into the latter, the diagnostic has failed.

**Energetic/material constraint should stay out unless it survives narrow wording.** The thermodynamic foundation is confirmed. But the diagnostic is strongest with four clean mechanism-confirmed claims. Adding energetic constraint as secondary context risks making the packet dependent on a fifth claim that requires more qualification than the other four. Reviewer Question 4 addresses this directly.

**Future review must preserve the split or the family should stay out.** The split is what makes the diagnostic reviewable. If in a future review the split is abandoned — if the diagnostic is widened to include the fork structure, the five-capacity architecture, or civilisational optionality — the family should be pulled from review rather than promoted on weaker terms. A narrow honest entry is more valuable than a broad dishonest one.

**Adjacent promoted families must not lend false certainty.** Legitimacy's strong evidence about authority-consequence coupling does not strengthen the compression-attractor claim. Relational-topology's empirical base does not validate "scaffolded relationality." Human-economy's commitment evidence does not confirm that a new coordination grammar is needed at the civilisational level. Each family stands on its own evidence.
