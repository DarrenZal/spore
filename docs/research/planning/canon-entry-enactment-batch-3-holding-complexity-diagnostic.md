If quoted or paraphrased, it must not expand to imply the five-capacity architecture, the fork framing, or any other excluded element.

If a future document treats the diagnostic promotion as endorsement of the five-capacity package, the fork structure, or civilisational optionality, the enactment's boundary has been violated.

Design contributions are tagged and separated from confirmed mechanism. The holding-complexity diagnostic maintains this standard.
