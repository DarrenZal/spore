## 6. Immediate Result

**What is now canon-facing that was not before:**

The economic rationale for why Spore's grammar treats commitments as the binding coordination primitive. Specifically: wage and transaction grammar fails under discovery because specifiability, containment, and measurability assumptions break; governance structure rather than contract terms becomes necessary; participation requires standing; and governance should vary by epistemic character of work.

**What remains outside canon:**

- The four-type commitment-signature taxonomy (delivery, discovery, stewardship, risk) — design hypothesis
- Standing-plus-differentiation composition — design hypothesis
- CLC as independent corroboration — excluded; CLC is design lineage
- Governed renegotiation as a named feature — design contribution
- Discovery-specific intensification — Johar's extension beyond confirmed sources
- Operational viability of commitment-based coordination — production evidence, not confirmed mechanism
- The Spore grammar parallel as confirming evidence — design context only

**What still remains explicitly qualified in the broader family:**

The synthesis note retains its full convergence account, claim register, open questions, and the Spore parallel. The body text's disposition section still describes the family's limitations (Johar-derived sources, untested commitment-economy parallel). The promoted status records that the confirmed layer has entered canon, not that the entire synthesis is validated.

## 9. Guardrails

- **Enactment does not validate the whole synthesis note.** The enacted paragraph carries only three confirmed mechanisms. The synthesis note's full convergence account, four-type taxonomy, standing-plus-differentiation composition, and Spore parallel remain research-level content, not canon.

- **The broader human-economy family still contains design contribution and hypothesis beyond canon.** Johar's specific formulations (commitment-type governance, governed renegotiation, "standing" as a named primitive) are design contributions. The four-type taxonomy and standing-plus-differentiation composition are design hypotheses. None of these entered canon with this enactment.

- **Future reuse must preserve the narrow enacted layer.** Any document that references the human-economy canon entry should reference the confirmed mechanisms only. The broader synthesis note may be cited for context but must not be treated as canon-validated.

- **Production precedent must not be rewritten as corroboration.** CLC demonstrates that commitment-based coordination works at production scale. This remains operational precedent and design lineage. If Spore's own operation generates production evidence for the confirmed mechanisms, that evidence must be evaluated independently, not retrofitted as confirming the theory.

- **Later batch work should preserve the same evidentiary discipline.** The holding-complexity diagnostic split (if pursued) and any future promotion batch must maintain the same separation between confirmed mechanism, design contribution, and design hypothesis. The standard is: can a future reader reconstruct the correct evidentiary layer from the canon text alone?
