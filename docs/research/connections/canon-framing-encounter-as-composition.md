# Canon Framing — Encounter as Composition (ADR-0055, Option D + E.1)

Shared framing for the post-Phase-3b canon-rebuild decision admitting `encounter` as derived glossary slug (ADR-0055). The derived-slug earned its admission through the ADR-0048-mandated dedicated earning-test evaluation, which honestly found that Encounter decomposes into existing verb-compositions within bounded Field-conditions rather than naming a missing primitive. This note articulates the compositional decomposition, names the Johar-native structured-encounter-as-discipline reading at discipline-layer, and preserves the federation-encounter pattern-library pathway under operationally-falsifiable triggers.

## 1. The insight in one paragraph

**Encounter names a coordination-event-scope-shape, not a missing coordination verb.** When multiple holons co-present in a shared temporal-spatial window — a BKC/Octo quarterly federation meeting, a Poietic Match match-event, a DW design-review, a cross-federation compose-event — the coordination that happens *inside* the window is Signal + Joint-commitment + Intent + Evidence firing within bounded Field-conditions (temporal window, spatial or virtual co-location, rule-in-use governing the event). The event-scope-shape itself is not doing coordination work beyond hosting the verbs; what makes the event feel like a distinct coordination-unit is the *quality of verb-composition discipline* the participants bring. Johar's "structured encounter" names this discipline-quality. Admitting `encounter` as derived glossary slug plus framing-note preserves canon-legibility for this compositional pattern without primitive-inflation; parking federation-encounter as pattern-library candidate under triggers E-1 through E-5 preserves the future-admission pathway under operational pressure.

## 2. Why derived-slug + framing-note + parked-pattern (three moves together)

The three moves address three distinct aspects of the Encounter-candidate structure:

- **Derived glossary slug** (`encounter`): anchors the term so references across bridge-notes, research docs, and future ADRs can cite a canonical definition. Without the slug, "encounter" floats as unanchored prose across the Johar corpus + operational discourse. Parallel to `attestation-of-execution` / `permeability` / `double-boundary` / `reciprocity` / `trust` — derived analytic vocabulary that does NOT introduce a new canon-object-class.
- **Framing-note** (this file): articulates *how* Encounter decomposes into existing verb-compositions within bounded Field-conditions, *why* the decomposition honors Johar-native structured-encounter discipline without primitive-inflation, and *what* residues remain canonically-acknowledged rather than silently routed-around (R-Enc-1 through R-Enc-4 per ADR-0055).
- **Parked pattern-library candidate with triggers**: reserves the future-admission pathway for federation-encounter as pattern-library entry, operationally-falsifiable via five triggers (E-1 pattern-library-spec declared; E-2 multi-instance operational-pressure reports; E-3 cross-tradition convergence; E-4 operational grammar-insufficiency; E-5 Field-sub-stratification separate-plan advances). Prevents silent-routing-around of the recurring-structure observation while honestly declining pattern-library admission today on pattern-library-spec-embryonic-state grounds.

All three moves together honor the honest earning-test outcome: Encounter is neither-a-primitive-now nor-inadmissible-forever; it is a canonical-compositional-structure with a triggered-admission-pathway at pattern-library layer.

## 3. Encounter decomposition — four elements within bounded Field-conditions

### 3.1 Signal (invitation + reframing transmission)

**Pre-event**: the invitation-signal transmits the convening-notice to candidate participants (federation-members, match-eligible-parties, team-members). Structurally: a Signal from the convener / convening-structure to the candidate-participant set.

**In-event**: signals circulate as live perturbation — reframing, dissent, shared-observation, emergent-salience. Expressive-power mode (ADR-0048) operates here; Signal carries articulation of dissent and reframing as part of the constructed-power substrate.

**Post-event**: closing-signals transmit dispersal, transition-to-next-phase, or handoff-to-external-channel.

Tradition anchors: Distributed Systems message-passing; KOI federation-signal-propagation; Ostrom collective-choice rule-invocation-signaling; Johar pre-reactive-signal-propagation.

Canonical home: existing Signal primitive. No new machinery. Derived-slug `encounter` does not duplicate Signal's role; it names the event-scope-shape within which Signal fires as one composition-element.

### 3.2 Joint-commitment (attendance-pledge + shared-orientation)

**Pre-event**: attendance-pledge is a joint-commitment (ADR-0050) — multiple parties jointly committing to be co-present at a specific scheduled time and place. This is a clear irreducibly-joint case: each attendance-pledge references the others' attendance-pledges, and the jointness-structure is load-bearing (a meeting with no joint-attendance-commitment is a broadcast, not an encounter).

**In-event**: shared-orientation / joint-attention is a joint-commitment at a different-but-compatible layer — participants jointly commit to attending the same situation (agenda-item, decision-point, shared-observation-context). Gilbert-pluralization-of-subject pattern.

**Post-event**: joint-commitments formed during the event (shared decisions, ratified protocol-adoptions, agreed-on-next-steps) are the primary coordination-artifact the event generates. ADR-0050's paradigm case — federation-protocol-version-adoption — typically happens *within* an encounter, not as a disembodied joint-commitment-formation-act.

Tradition anchors: Gilbert *Joint Commitment* 2013; Tuomela we-mode; Searle collective-intentionality (we-intend); Bratman shared-intention.

Canonical home: existing Joint-commitment primitive (ADR-0050). Derived-slug `encounter` names the event-scope-shape within which joint-commitment fires as a second composition-element.

### 3.3 Intent (pre-event surfacing + in-event shaping)

**Pre-event**: participants surface intents that motivate their attendance — what they want the encounter to accomplish, what constraints they bring, what they're looking for from the other parties. Structurally: Intent declarations that the invitation-signal or a pre-event-coordination-surface collects.

**In-event**: intents are shaped through conversation — articulated, re-surfaced, compromised, discovered. Expressive-power mode (ADR-0048) operates here; Intent carries articulation of meaning, desire, and dissent.

**Post-event**: new intents emerge from the encounter as coordination-artifact — participants leave with changed / new intents shaped by what they learned in the event.

Tradition anchors: Bratman planning-theory; REA/ValueFlows intent-as-promise-antecedent; Johar "intent pressure" gap-measurement; commons-governance collective-choice-rule-proposal.

Canonical home: existing Intent primitive. Derived-slug `encounter` names the event-scope-shape within which Intent fires as a third composition-element.

### 3.4 Evidence (in-event attestation + post-event capture)

**In-event**: participants attest observations, contributions, and decisions as they happen — real-time evidence-capture via recording, meeting-notes, shared document updates, or informal witness-signaling.

**Post-event**: evidence-capture artifacts (meeting-minutes, decision-records, agreement-documents, recording-summaries) are the primary durable-evidence-artifact the event generates. Attestation-of-execution (ADR-0053) subset applies — evidence of *whether* and *in what state* the event's constitutive commitments and decisions were reached.

Tradition anchors: Distributed Systems cryptographic provenance; REA EconomicEvent; Governance-Process W3C CR exit / IETF interop reports; commons-governance monitoring-rule; Johar situational-truthing depth-of-encounter.

Canonical home: existing Evidence primitive + `attestation-of-execution` derived-slug (ADR-0053). Derived-slug `encounter` names the event-scope-shape within which Evidence fires as a fourth composition-element.

### 3.5 Field-conditions (bounded temporal-spatial scope)

What distinguishes encounter-hosted verb-compositions from general verb-compositions is the **bounded temporal-spatial scope** — the event has a start-time, an end-time, a physical-or-virtual location, and typically rule-in-use scaffolding (agenda, facilitation protocol, decision-rule) governing what verbs can fire and under what conditions.

These are **Field-conditions**, not a separate primitive. ADR-0046's Field rule-level stratification already hosts rule-in-use (the working-rule participants reference when explaining their actions); the scheduled-window + location + event-specific-rules of an encounter are operational-level rules-in-use scoped to the event.

**Co-presence-establish structure** (common-knowledge of mutual attention; Lewis / Schiffer): this is substrate-like rather than verb-like (per ADR-0055 Step 0.5 finding). The participants' mutual-attention to the shared situation is a *field-condition* in which verbs fire, not a coordination-act performed *by* agents. This is the R-Enc-1 residue per ADR-0055 — genuinely-distinct-but-substrate-like structure that a future Field sub-stratification may admit as Field-condition type (Trigger E-5), but which ADR-0055 declines to admit now on single-tradition-support grounds (Johar + common-knowledge-philosophy is not cross-tradition-convergent enough to warrant Field-sub-stratification).

## 4. Johar-native structured-encounter as discipline, not primitive

The Johar corpus (7 bridge-notes) uses "encounter" in four structurally-distinct ways, none of which warrants primitive-admission:

### 4.1 Structured encounter as protocol-design-practice

johar-situational-truthing: *"Structured encounter as protocol — Johar's 'disciplined conversation' maps to KOI's federation protocol design. What does it mean for two Spore nodes (with different partial graphs) to formally 'encounter' each other and produce a provisional stabilisation? This is a protocol design question."*

This is **discipline-at-protocol-layer**: a quality of how federation-protocol-invocation events should be designed. Not a missing verb; a design-practice applied to existing verbs (Signal + Joint-commitment + Intent + Evidence firing under structured-conversation discipline). Analogous to how `care-commoning` (ADR-0045) admits care-ethics as cross-cutting doctrine rather than as primitive — the practice-disposition operates across primitives without being one.

### 4.2 Encounter-rules as architectural metacognition

johar-metacognition-stack: *"encounter rules"* at Layer 10 Architectural — *"design of encounter rules, trust weights, federation protocol IS architectural metacognition."*

This is **rule-in-use at the encounter-scope-level**: the rules-in-use (ADR-0046) governing how encounter-hosted verb-compositions unfold. Encounter-rules are a *type* of rule-in-use; they apply to the operational-rule level within the ADR-0046 3-level stack, scoped to event-bounded-Field-conditions. Not a primitive; a canonical-home-already-in-ADR-0046.

### 4.3 Federated encounter events at constructed layer

johar-power-cannot-be-allocated: *"federated encounter events"* as where *"actors across nodes encounter real situations and build coherence through engagement."* Constructed-power mode (ADR-0048).

This is **constructed-power-mode-firing** — situational agency emerging dynamically through verb-loop-compression under real-situation exposure. Already-named canon-object-class (mode-across-primitives per ADR-0048). Admitting `encounter` as primitive would reopen ADR-0048's explicit naming of emergence as mode-not-primitive.

### 4.4 Encounter-engineering as discipline preserving perceptual-relational capacity

johar-ecology-of-courage + johar-miss-engineered-city: *"encounter engineering as related concepts that describe threats to and conditions for maintaining these capacities"*; *"infrastructure that shapes encounter determines whether agents can exercise the perceptual and relational capacities coordination requires."*

This is **infrastructure-design-discipline** — how the architectures around encounters (physical spaces, digital platforms, federation-protocol-UX) enable or degrade the care-courage-and-presence capacities coordination requires. Canonical home is care-commoning doctrine (ADR-0045) + potentially future four-enabling-conditions pattern-library entry (ADR-0048 parking). Not a primitive.

## 5. Pattern-library admission triggers (summary)

ADR-0055 parks federation-encounter as pattern-library admission candidate with five operationally-falsifiable triggers (full formulation in ADR-0055 §Maturation triggers):

- **E-1**: Pattern-library-spec declared (infrastructure-gate)
- **E-2**: 3+ independent instance-family reports of ad-hoc federation-encounter re-implementation (operational-pressure-gate)
- **E-3**: 3+ independent cross-tradition convergence on event-discipline / co-presence-design / federation-event-engineering (cross-tradition-gate)
- **E-4**: Instance-family field-report of grammar-insufficiency around event-recurrence (insufficiency-evidence-gate)
- **E-5**: Field sub-stratification separate-plan advances to co-admission-pairing state (structural-coupling-gate)

Any one trigger firing opens a future federation-encounter pattern-library admission cycle.

## 6. Canonically-acknowledged residues

### R-Enc-1 — Co-presence-establish substrate-irreducibility remains implicit

Step 0.5 finding: common-knowledge-of-mutual-attention (Lewis / Schiffer / Bacharach) is genuinely-distinct-but-substrate-like. ADR-0055 declines Field sub-stratification admission on single-tradition-support grounds. Trigger E-5 preserves the future-admission pathway.

### R-Enc-2 — Structured-encounter-as-event-discipline category-fit open

Johar-native structured-encounter is closest to an event-discipline; canon-object-class for event-disciplines (distinct from doctrines / modes / patterns) is not currently declared. ADR-0055 declines canon-object-class inflation for single-candidate presence. Future canon-review recurrence-based admission if additional event-discipline candidates surface.

### R-Enc-3 — Protocol-version-adoption overlap with joint-commitment

ADR-0050 named federation-protocol-version-adoption as paradigm irreducibly-joint case. The overlap is partial: protocol-adoption-moment is a *content* covered by joint-commitment; the *event-structure* hosting it is the framing-note-articulated composition. Two readings coexist without conflict; not merged.

### R-Enc-4 — Pattern-library infrastructure under-specified

Deferring federation-encounter admission on pattern-library-spec-embryonic grounds is honest; the spec-gap is itself a canon-level concern. Future pattern-library-spec work may address E-1 and unlock federation-encounter admission.

## 8. For future readers

If you're reading this note trying to decide whether Spore has an "Encounter primitive": **no**. Spore has a 9-primitive roster (3 structural: field / holon / membrane; 6 verbs: intent / commitment / joint-commitment / evidence / signal / reproduction) as of 2026-04-22. `encounter` is a derived glossary slug anchoring a compositional-pattern articulated in this framing-note.

If you're reading this note trying to understand how to design a federation-event (BKC quarterly, PM match-event, DW design-review, cross-federation compose-event): the compositional structure is §3 above — Signal + Joint-commitment + Intent + Evidence within bounded Field-conditions — and the Johar-native structured-encounter discipline (§4) is how participants bring quality to the verb-compositions within the event. The operational-primitive-grammar is already Spore's existing primitive grammar; what makes an encounter work is *the discipline applied to composing those primitives under bounded Field-conditions*.

If you think federation-encounter should be a pattern-library entry: see the five triggers in ADR-0055 §Maturation triggers. Any one firing opens a re-evaluation cycle.
